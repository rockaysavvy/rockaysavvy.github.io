Here are a few of my favorite ways to add difficulty to the game.

SNAILL

Solo, No AI, Loud, all Loot. Just about the hardest way to engage with combat. Playing as Jupiter helps significantly with carrying bags.

Baker only

Complete the campaign by only using Baker to play missions.

No reloads

Complete loud missions by picking up weapons instead of reloading.

Machete only

Complete loud missions only using the Machete (or another melee weapon). Bonus points for playing as Danny Trejo.
