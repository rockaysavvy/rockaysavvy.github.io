Glizzy BEMR

The Glizzy BEMR is an epic automatic pistol, a heavily modified Glizzy with a custom “night fury” finish. It is a signature weapon of both Cracker and Peak. Despite its modifications, the Glizzy BEMR still has very low damage output compared to other high-quality secondary weapons.

Appearances

  • Cracker and Peak have Glizzy BEMRs when Baker hires them.
  • Elite hitmen may join Baker’s crew with Glizzy BEMRs.
  • In Baker’s Battle, elite thieves and gangsters may also bring Glizzy BEMRs when Baker hires them.
  • Cracker may carry a Glizzy BEMR in King's Treasure, Juiced Up, Iced Out, and Hauling Ash, Chase may carry one in New Blood, Mr. X may carry one in The Big Khan, and Peak may carry one in Hauling Ash.
  • The Graves may find Glizzy BEMRs when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Glizzy BEMRs to players of any respect level. In multiplayer, players who are at least Respected can buy Glizzy BEMRs from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Glizzy BEMR by completing a medium or long shuffle in multiplayer.

Trivia

  • The letters indicating the weapon’s modifications have been rearranged from their normal order of “EMBR” to spell “beamer,” slang for a highly accurate player or weapon.
