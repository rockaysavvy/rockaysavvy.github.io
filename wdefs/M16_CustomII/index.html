The Chopper EGH is an unlockable epic assault rifle. It consists of a heavily modified Chopper with a holo sight, an extended quickdraw magazine, and a vertical long foregrip, which offer significantly boosted performance over the Chopper. It performs similar to the TIMM 4 ERCG with immensely improved magazine capacity but worse reload speed and accuracy, rendering it a respectable choice among the unlockable assault rifles.

Appearances

  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Chopper EGH if he has level four of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Chopper EGHs to players of any respect level in Baker’s Battle.
