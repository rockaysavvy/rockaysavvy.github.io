Sting Elite O

Magazine size35

The Sting Elite O is an unlockable rare submachine gun. It consists of a heavily modified Sting with an extended quickdraw magazine and a 3x scope, which provide substantially improved performance. It behaves like the SMG 9mm with better magazine capacity but worse accuracy and overall damage output, rendering it an acceptable choice of rare weapon.

Appearances

  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Sting Elite O if he has level three of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Sting Elite Os to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Players who are at least connected can also purchase Sting Elite Os from the black market.
