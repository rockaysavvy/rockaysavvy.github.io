The Sting Elite is a rare SMG, a modified Sting E with improved performance. Even with the enhancements, it remains a weak primary weapon.

Appearances

  • Elite thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with Sting Elites.
  • In Baker’s Battle, veteran thieves and gangsters as well as elite hitmen may also bring Sting Elites when Baker hires them.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Sting Elite if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Sting Elites when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Sting Elites to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Players who are at least Connected can purchase Sting Elites from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Sting Elite by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.
