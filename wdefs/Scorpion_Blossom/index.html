The legendary Scorp is a modified Scorp with the same name. It has significantly improved performance and features a cherry-blossom branch affixed to its receiver. It is one of Volk’s signature weapons.

Appearances

  • Volk has a Scorp when Baker hires him.
  • Volk can carry a Scorp in Juiced Up, Iced Out.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Scorp if he has level three of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Scorps when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Scorps to Respected or higher players. In multiplayer, Formidable or higher players can buy Scorps from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Scorp by completing a long shuffle in multiplayer.
