The blossom Scorp is a legendary submachine gun and Volk’s signature primary weapon, comprising a modified Scorp which offers significantly boosted performance. It is similar to the SMG 9mm ERG with better overall damage output but worse accuracy, magazine capacity, and handling, rendering it a reasonable choice of submachine gun.

Appearances

  • Volk uses a Scorp.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Scorp if he has level five of Super Strapped.
