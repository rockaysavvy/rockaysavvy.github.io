The Fire Extinguisher is an uncommon special primary weapon, which heisters can pick up during missions and use to put out fires. It does no damage and cannot be purchased from the black market or Arms Dealer.

Appearances

  • Heisters can find Extinguishers in some ambulances, trucks, and taxis in Truck Convoys.
  • Extinguishers appear throughout Rockay Central Station in Money Train.
