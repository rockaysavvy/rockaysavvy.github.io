Swiss

Range1000
Accuracy2.6
Magazine size8

The Swiss is a common semiautomatic pistol. Its easy availability is its strongest asset, since its small magazine and low damage render it an underwhelming choice of sidearm.

Appearances

  • The pilot in Jet Set and the Viet Cong sniper in Pinned Down wield Swisses.
  • There are several Swisses in the weapons locker in Break Out.
  • Ranger has a Swiss when he joins Baker’s crew.
  • Detective Phips and the chief guard have Swisses.
  • Security guards, police officers, park rangers, prison guards, and Tier I–III SWAT officers and shields all carry Swisses.
  • Tier I–III Chicos, Riders, and Scudos as well as Tier I backup can wield Swisses.
  • Chico, Rider, and Scudo captains as well as Chico, Rider, Scudo, and Sicario snipers carry Swisses.
  • Chicos, Sicarios, Scudos, Riders, and East Men can have Swisses in story missions.
  • Rookie thieves and gangsters have Swisses when Baker hires them. In multiplayer, veteran thieves and gangsters can as well.
  • Low respect and Connected players can purchase Swisses from the black market.

Trivia

  • The Swiss is based on the SIG Sauer P220.
  • The name “Swiss” comes from the English translation of SIG, “Swiss Industrial Company.”
