Swiss

Range1000

Magazine size8

The Swiss is an unlockable common pistol and a signature weapon of Axe, Cage, Midnight, and Slick, comparable to the Brescia 92. It is an excellent choice of common weapon due to its impressive reload speed, accuracy, and overall damage output.

Appearances

  • Axe, Cage, Midnight, and Slick all wield Swisses.
  • Detective Phips uses a Swiss, except in story missions.
  • The jet pilot in Jet Set wields a Swiss.
  • Some of the police officers in Break Out use Swisses.
  • Some prison guards in Baller carry Swisses.
  • There are several Swisses in the RCPD weapon lockers in Break Out.
  • Ranger has a Swiss when he joins Baker’s crew in the campaign.
  • One of the prison guards in Baller drops a Swiss when he dies.
  • Elite security guards, police officers, elite police officers, park rangers, and tier I–II SWAT officers all wield Swisses, and tier I–III Chicos, tier I Graves, tier I–III Riders, tier I–III Scudos, chief guards, prison guards, and security guards can also have them.
  • Tier I–III Chico, Rider, Scudo, Sicario, and SWAT snipers always wield Swisses.
  • Tier I–III SWAT shields have Swisses.
  • In turf wars, tier I–III Chicos always carry Swisses, and some tier I–III Riders and Scudos also wield them.
  • Tier I–III Chico and Scudo captains carry Swisses.
  • In story missions, East Men, Viet Cong snipers, Viet Cong soldiers, officers, prison guards, and park rangers use Swisses, and Chicos, Riders, Scudos, and Sicarios may also use them.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect and connected players can purchase Swisses from the black market in the campaign.
  • Generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign have Swisses.

Trivia

  • The Swiss is based on the SIG Sauer P220.
  • The name “Swiss” comes from the English translation of SIG, “Swiss Industrial Company.”
