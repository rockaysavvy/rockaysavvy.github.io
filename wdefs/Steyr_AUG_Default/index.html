The Sturm GWR is an unlockable epic assault rifle, comparable to the Chopper R+. It’s among the most effective assault rifles, because its exceptional accuracy, close-range damage output, and handling make up for its inferior reload speed.

Appearances

  • Stinger carries a Sturm GWR in Silence is Gold.
  • Players can find Sturm GWRs in the weapons vaults in compound basements.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Sturm GWR if he has level four of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Sturm GWRs to players of any respect level in Baker’s Battle.

Trivia

  • The Sturm GWR is based on the Steyr AUG.
  • “Sturmgewehr” means assault rifle in German, though it typically refers to a different firearm.
