Theresa’s SMG is a unique epic submachine gun. It consists of a heavily modified Micro SMG with a suppressor, an extended magazine, and a holo sight, which offer significantly boosted performance over the Micro SMG. It resembles the Kellgern 9 Elite with better overall damage output but somewhat worse damage per shot, making it a balanced choice among weapons.

Appearances

  • Theresa has this weapon in Silence is Gold and Who Took the Dogs Out?.
