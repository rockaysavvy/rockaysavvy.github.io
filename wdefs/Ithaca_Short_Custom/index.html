The Lupara Mk. II is an unlockable uncommon shotgun, a modified Lupara Short which provides substantially improved performance, comparable to the 12 Gauge. Even with its impressive damage per shot, its awful controllability, accuracy, magazine capacity, and overall damage output make it a weak uncommon weapon.

Appearances

  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Lupara Mk. II if he has level two of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Lupara Mk. IIs when they capture a turf.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect, connected, and respected players can purchase Lupara Mk. IIs from the black market in the campaign.
  • Uncommon generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign may have Lupara Mk. IIs.
