The Vintovka U EMB is an epic assault rifle, a heavily modified Vintovka with a shorter barrel and a larger magazine. It represents a further improvement in damage output over the Vintovka U MB.

Appearances

  • Elite hitmen may join Baker’s crew with Vintovka U EMBs.
  • In Baker’s Battle, elite thieves and gangsters may also bring Vintovka U EMBs when Baker hires them.
  • Tulip can carry a Vintovka U EMB in Secret Symphony.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Vintovka U EMB if he has level two of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Vintovka U EMBs when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Vintovka U EMBs to players of any respect level. In multiplayer, Respected or higher players can buy Vintovka U EMBs from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Vintovka U EMB by completing a medium or long shuffle in multiplayer.
