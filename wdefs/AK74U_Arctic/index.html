The Vintovka U EOMB is a legendary assault rifle, a heavily modified Vintovka U which provides significantly boosted performance. It performs like the TIMM 4 EOSG with improved magazine capacity and close-range damage output but slightly worse accuracy and controllability, making it a reasonable choice of assault rifle.
