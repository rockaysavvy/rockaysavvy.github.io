The Kellgern 9 E is an uncommon modified Kellgern 9 equipped with a 50-round magazine.

Appearances

  • Veteran thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with a Kellgern 9 E.
  • In multiplayer, elite thieves and gangsters may also have Kellgern 9 Es when Baker hires them.
  • The Graves may find Kellgern 9 Es when they re-take a turf they have previously captured.
  • Low respect, Connected, and Respected players can purchase Kellgern 9 Es from the black market, as can campaign players who are Formidable.
  • Players can also obtain a Kellgern 9 E by completing a short shuffle in multiplayer.
