Reaper’s Saw

$35,000–50,000
Damage135
RPM800
Range3000
Accuracy3.5
Magazine size100

The Reaper’s Saw is an unlockable epic machine gun from the Heavy Hitters Pack and Lector’s signature primary weapon. It is similar to the Machinegun with enormously better accuracy but worse reload speed and damage per shot, rendering it a reasonable choice of weapon.

Appearances

  • Players who own the Heavy Hitters Pack automatically unlock the Reaper’s Saw in multiplayer and receive a Reaper’s Saw at the beginning of every campaign playthrough.
  • Lector wields a Reaper’s Saw.
  • Heisters can find a Reaper’s Saws in a case beside the weapons truck in motel assassinations.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Reaper’s Saw if he has level four of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Reaper’s Saws to players of any respect level in Baker’s Battle.

Trivia

  • The Reaper’s Saw is based on the M249 Para.
  • “SAW” stands for Squad Automatic Weapon.

Known issues

  • The folded bipod in the Reaper’s Saw’s icon intersects its foregrip.
