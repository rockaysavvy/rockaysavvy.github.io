The Reaper's Saw is an epic LMG from the Heavy Hitters Pack, a modified SAW Para with a box magazine. Its large ammunition pool compensates for its relatively restrained damage output.

Appearances

  • Players who own the Heavy Hitters Pack receive a Reaper's Saw at the beginning of each campaign playthrough.
  • Elite hitmen may join Baker’s crew with Reaper's Saws.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Reaper's Saw if he has level two of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Reaper's Saws when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Reaper's Saws to players of any respect level. In multiplayer, players who are at least Respected can buy Reaper's Saws from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Reaper's Saw by completing a medium or long shuffle in multiplayer.
