PT 22

Damage90

Range1000

Magazine size6

The PT 22 is a unique epic semiautomatic pistol. It is one of Runaway’s signature weapons. Its low damage output and tiny magazine render it near-useless in combat, but Runaway can use it to take out guards and cameras silently. Plus, it’s adorable.

Appearances

  • Runaway has a PT 22 when Baker hires her.
  • Runaway may carry a PT 22 in Silence is Gold and The Big Khan.

Trivia

  • The PT 22 is based on the Taurus PT22.
