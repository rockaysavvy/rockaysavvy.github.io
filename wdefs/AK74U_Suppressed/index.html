The Vintovka U RS is an unlockable epic assault rifle. It consists of a heavily modified Vintovka U with a suppressor, a red dot sight, and an extended quickdraw magazine, which offer substantially improved performance over the Vintovka U. It behaves like the TIMM 4 EOSG with notably improved overall damage output and reload speed but worse accuracy and controllability, making it a first-rate option among the unlockable assault rifles.

Appearances

  • A Vintovka U RS appears in the background of the multiplayer menu.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Vintovka U RS if he has level four of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Vintovka U RSs to players of any respect level in Baker’s Battle. Respected, formidable, godfather, and crime boss players can also purchase Vintovka U RSs from the black market.
