The Vintovka U RS is a legendary assault rifle, a suppressed modified Vintovka with a shortened barrel and a red dot sight. It’s a versatile choice for heisters, since it excels at stealth eliminations but also has sufficient damage output to hold its own in a prolonged firefight.

Appearances

  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Vintovka U RS if he has level three of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Vintovka U RSs when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Vintovka U RSs to players who are at least Respected. In multiplayer, players who are at least Formidable can buy Vintovka U RSs from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Vintovka U RS by completing a long shuffle in multiplayer.
