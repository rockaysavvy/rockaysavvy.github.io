The SLR G is a rare DMR. It is a modified SLR equipped with an angled foregrip, which somewhat reduces recoil.

Appearances

  • Elite thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with SLR Gs.
  • In Baker’s Battle, veteran thieves and gangsters as well as elite hitmen may also bring SLR Gs when Baker hires them.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with an SLR G if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find SLR Gs when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell SLR Gs to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Players who are at least Connected can purchase SLR Gs from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain an SLR G by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.
