357 BOA

Magazine size6

The 357 BOA is a legendary hand cannon. Its high accuracy and damage per shot compensate for its small ammunition pool. It is a signature weapon of both Baker and Touchdown.

Appearances

  • Detective Donny carries a 357 BOA. Detective Phips also carries one in Break Out.
  • Baker and Touchdown begin multiplayer with 357 BOAs.
  • He also used to have a 357 BOA in Under Siege before it was removed.
  • Cracker may carry a 357 BOA in Juiced Up, Iced Out, and Tulip may carry one in Secret Symphony.
  • Baker begins his campaign with a 357 BOA.
  • The Graves may find 357 BOAs when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell 357 BOAs to Respected or higher players. In multiplayer, Formidable or higher players can buy 357 BOAs from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a 357 BOA by completing a long shuffle in multiplayer.
