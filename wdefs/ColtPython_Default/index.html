357 BOA

$30,300–38,500
Damage400
RPM260
Range1500
Accuracy1.4
Magazine size6

The 357 BOA is a legendary hand cannon and Travis Baker’s signature secondary weapon. It behaves like the YA-R3K with better damage per shot and controllability but worse magazine capacity and overall damage output, rendering it a poor choice of hand cannon.

Appearances

  • Tulip uses a 357 BOA in Secret Symphony.
  • Detective Donny carries a 357 BOA, except in story missions.
  • Detective Phips wields a 357 BOA in story missions.
  • Baker used to use a 357 BOA in Under Siege, before that mission was removed.
  • Baker begins his campaign with a 357 BOA.
