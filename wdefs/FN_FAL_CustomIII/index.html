The SLR EG is an epic DMR, a heavily modified SLR with a gold finish. Counterintuitively, it has lower damage output than the lower-quality SLR ER because it lacks a quick-draw magazine.

Appearances

  • Elite hitmen may join Baker’s crew with SLR EGs.
  • In the Baker’s Battle, elite gangsters may also bring SLR EGs when Baker hires them.
  • Forehand may carry an SLR EG in Secret Symphony, and Silver may carry one in Silence is Gold.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with an SLR EG if he has level two of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find SLR EGs when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell SLR EGs to players of any respect level. In multiplayer, Respected or higher players can buy SLR EGs from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain an SLR EG by completing a medium or long shuffle in multiplayer.

Trivia

  • The SLR EG should probably be called the “SLR EGR,” because it has a red dot sight.
