Bearslayer

Damage380
RPM300
Range1500
Accuracy1.3
Magazine size6

The Bearslayer is a unique legendary hand cannon from Baker’s Bundle. It performs similar to the 357 BOA with improved overall damage output but slightly worse damage per shot and handling, making it a subpar choice among secondary weapons.

Appearances

  • Players who own Baker’s Bundle automatically unlock the Bearslayer in multiplayer and can purchase a Bearslayer at the beginning of every campaign playthrough.

Trivia

  • The Bearslayer is based on the Ruger Super Redhawk Alaskan, a bear defense gun.
