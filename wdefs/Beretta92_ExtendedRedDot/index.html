The Brescia 92 ER is an unlockable rare pistol. It comprises a heavily modified Brescia 92 with an extended magazine and a red dot sight, which offer significantly boosted performance similar to the Swiss Elite ES. With its solid reload speed, overall damage output, and accuracy, it’s a fantastic choice of rare weapon.

Appearances

  • Clutch has a Brescia 92 ER in Who Took the Dogs Out?, Forehand uses one in Secret Symphony, and Ropes wields one in New Blood and Juiced Up, Iced Out.
  • Some Skeety Kitty Arms locations sell Brescia 92 ERs.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Brescia 92 ERs to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Connected or higher players can also purchase Brescia 92 ERs from the black market.
