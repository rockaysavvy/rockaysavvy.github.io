The Slate 36 is an unlockable rare assault rifle from the Tactical Weapon Pack. It resembles the Sniper Rifle with hugely improved overall damage output and handling but hugely worse damage per shot, making it an inferior option among the unlockable assault rifles.

Appearances

  • Players who own the Tactical Weapon Pack automatically unlock the Slate 36 in multiplayer and receive a Slate 36 at the beginning of every campaign playthrough.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Slate 36 if he has level three of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Slate 36s to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Players who are at least connected can also purchase Slate 36s from the black market.

Trivia

  • The Slate 36 is based on the Heckler & Koch SL8, a civilian variant of the G36.
  • The name “Slate” comes from reading “SL8” as SMS language.
