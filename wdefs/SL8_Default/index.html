Slate 36

$24,000–26,400
Damage260
RPM350
Range3000
Accuracy1.4
Magazine size10

The Slate 36 is a rare DMR. Its unusually small magazine limits its combat effectiveness, despite its high damage per shot.

Appearances

  • Players who own the Tactical Weapon Pack receive a Slate 36 at the beginning of each campaign playthrough.
  • Elite thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with Slate 36s.
  • In Baker’s Battle, veteran thieves and gangsters as well as elite hitmen may also bring Slate 36s when Baker hires them.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Slate 36 if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Slate 36s when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Slate 36s to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Connected or higher players can purchase Slate 36s from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Slate 36 by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.

Trivia

  • The Slate 36 is based on the Heckler & Koch SL8, a civilian variant of the G36.
  • The name “Slate” comes from reading “SL8” as SMS language.
