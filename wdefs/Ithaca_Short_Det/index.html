The Lupara Detective Short is a unique uncommon shotgun, in the same class as the Pump. It is one of the worst weapons, because its respectable damage per shot fails to make up for its abysmal handling, accuracy, magazine capacity, overall damage output, and reload speed. Players cannot purchase it from the black market or Arms Dealer, but they can pick it up if they encounter it during a mission.
