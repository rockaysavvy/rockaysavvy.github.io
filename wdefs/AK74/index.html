Vintovka

$11,000–13,200
Damage105
RPM600
Range2300
Accuracy2.5
Magazine size30

The Vintovka is an unlockable uncommon assault rifle, comparable to the SAM-AF1. Its terrible accuracy and overall damage output make it a poor choice of assault rifle.

Appearances

  • The Viet Cong sniper in Pinned Down wields a Vintovka.
  • Some Skeety Kitty Arms locations sell Vintovkas.
  • A Vintovka appears in the background of the multiplayer menu.
  • The boss props up Vintovkas next to his throne at certain levels.
  • Tier II Graves, tier III Cagnali soldiers, and tier II Riders can wield Vintovkas.
  • In turf attacks and defenses, some tier II Riders have Vintovkas.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Vintovka if he has level two of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Vintovkas when they capture a turf.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect, connected, and respected players can purchase Vintovkas from the black market in the campaign.
  • Uncommon generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign sometimes carry Vintovkas.

Trivia

  • The Vintovka is based on the AK-74.
  • “Vintovka” means “rifle” in Russian.
