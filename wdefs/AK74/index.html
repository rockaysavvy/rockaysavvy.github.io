The Vintovka is a rare assault rifle. Its low damage output for an assault rifle renders it ineffective in combat.

Appearances

  • The Viet Cong sniper in Pinned Down uses a Vintovka.
  • Tier II Riders and backup can carry Vintovkas.
  • Elite thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with Vintovkas.
  • In Baker’s Battle, veteran thieves and gangsters as well as elite hitmen may also bring Vintovkas when Baker hires them.
  • Some Skeety Kitty Arms locations sell Vintovkas.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Vintovka if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Vintovkas when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Vintovkas to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Players who are at least Connected can purchase Vintovkas from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Vintovka by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.

Trivia

  • The Vintovka is based on the AK-74.
  • “Vintovka” means “rifle” in Russian.
