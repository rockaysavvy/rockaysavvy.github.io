The BU-RP R is an epic SMG, a modified BU-RP with a red dot sight.

Appearances

  • Elite hitmen may join Baker’s crew with BU-RP Rs.
  • In Baker’s Battle, elite thieves and gangsters may also bring BU-RP Rs when Baker hires them.
  • Ropes can carry a BU-RP R in Juiced Up, Iced Out.
  • Some Skeety Kitty Arms locations sell BU-RP Rs.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a BU-RP R if he has level two of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find BU-RP Rs when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell BU-RP Rs to players of any respect level. In multiplayer, players who are at least Respected can buy BU-RP Rs from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a BU-RP R by completing a medium or long shuffle in multiplayer.
