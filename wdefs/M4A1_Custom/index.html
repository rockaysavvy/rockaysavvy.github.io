The TIMM 4 ERCG is an unlockable legendary assault rifle and Ranger’s signature primary weapon, comprising a heavily modified TIMM 4 which provides substantially improved performance. It’s like the Chopper R+ with enormously better magazine capacity but worse damage per shot and reload speed, making it a first-rate choice of assault rifle.

Appearances

  • Lector uses a TIMM 4 ERCG in Who Took the Dogs Out?.
  • Ranger has a TIMM 4 ERCG when he joins Baker’s crew in the campaign. He also carries one in multiplayer.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a TIMM 4 ERCG if he has level five of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell TIMM 4 ERCGs to respected, formidable, godfather, and crime boss players in Baker’s Battle.
