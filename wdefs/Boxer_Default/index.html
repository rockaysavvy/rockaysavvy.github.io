The Boxer is a common blunt melee weapon. Its low damage per hit makes it the second-weakest melee weapon, after Fists, and the weakest that players can purchase.

Appearances

  • Rookie thieves and gangsters may have Boxers when Baker hires them.
  • Baker begins his campaign with a Boxer if he doesn’t have Super Strapped.
  • Low respect and Connected players can purchase Boxers from the black market.
