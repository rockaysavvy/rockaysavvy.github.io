Glizzy

$3,500–4,200
Damage85
RPM1000
Range1000
Accuracy4.0
Magazine size17

The Glizzy is an unlockable uncommon pistol and Macho’s signature secondary weapon, similar to the Bohema. Notwithstanding its solid handling, its inferior accuracy and overall damage output make it an ineffective pistol.

Appearances

  • Macho wields a Glizzy.
  • There is a Glizzy next to Jupiter on the bar in The Blue Bull.
  • A Glizzy appears in the background of the multiplayer menu.
  • A Glizzy lies beside the murder victim in some Strip Mall Robberies.
  • Some tier I Graves, chief guards, and security guards use Glizzies.
  • In story missions, jet pilots wield Glizzies, and Arctic soldiers can also have them.
  • The Graves may find Glizzies when they capture a turf.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect, connected, and respected players can purchase Glizzies from the black market in the campaign.
  • Uncommon generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign may have Glizzies.

Trivia

  • The Glizzy is based on the Glock 18.
  • “Glizzy” is DMV slang for Glock.
