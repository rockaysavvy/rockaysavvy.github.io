Glizzy

$3,500–4,200
Damage65
RPM1000
Range1000
Accuracy4.5
Magazine size17

The Glizzy is an uncommon automatic pistol. Its high rate of fire somewhat compensates for its abysmal damage per shot, but it remains one of the weakest secondary weapons available.

Appearances

  • Jupiter has a Glizzy when she joins Baker’s crew.
  • There is a Glizzy on the bar in The Blue Bull.
  • The henchmen in Hook Up have Glizzies.
  • Security guards, prison guards, and the chief guard can carry Glizzies.
  • Touchdown has a Glizzy when Baker’s army is Tier I.
  • Tier II backup may bring Glizzies.
  • Veteran thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with a Glizzy.
  • In multiplayer, elite thieves and gangsters may also have Glizzies when Baker hires them.
  • The Graves may find Glizzies when they re-take a turf they have previously captured.
  • Low respect, Connected, and Respected players can purchase Glizzies from the black market, as can campaign players who are Formidable.
  • Players can also obtain a Glizzy by completing a short shuffle in multiplayer.

Trivia

  • The Glizzy is based on the Glock 18.
  • “Glizzy” is DMV slang for Glock.
