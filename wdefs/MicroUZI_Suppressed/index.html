The Micro SMG ERS is an epic submachine gun. It consists of a heavily modified Micro SMG with a suppressor, an extended quickdraw magazine, and a red dot sight, which offer significantly boosted performance over the Micro SMG. It is comparable to the Kellgern 9 Elite with better overall damage output and reload speed but worse magazine capacity and damage per shot, making it an adequate choice among weapons.

Appearances

  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Micro SMG ERS if he has level four of Super Strapped.
