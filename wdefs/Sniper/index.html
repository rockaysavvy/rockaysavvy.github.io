The Sniper Rifle is an unlockable legendary sniper and Peak’s signature primary weapon, comparable to the Slate 36. With its superior specifications in general, it is an excellent choice of sniper.

Appearances

  • Peak wields a Sniper Rifle.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Sniper Rifle if he has level five of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Sniper Rifles to respected, formidable, godfather, and crime boss players in Baker’s Battle.
