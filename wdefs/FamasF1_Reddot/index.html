The SAM-AF1 R is an unlockable rare assault rifle from the Heavy Hitters Pack. It comprises a modified SAM-AF1 with a red dot sight which offers slightly improved performance. It is like the Vintovka U with improved magazine capacity but worse overall damage output, rendering it a below-average choice among the unlockable assault rifles.

Appearances

  • Players who own the Heavy Hitters Pack automatically unlock the SAM-AF1 R in multiplayer and receive an SAM-AF1 R at the beginning of every campaign playthrough.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with an SAM-AF1 R if he has level three of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell SAM-AF1 Rs to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Connected or higher players can also purchase SAM-AF1 Rs from the black market.
