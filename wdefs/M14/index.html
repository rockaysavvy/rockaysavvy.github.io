The Scout 14 is an epic marksman rifle. It resembles the SLR with immensely better damage per shot but immensely worse controllability and overall damage output, rendering it an inferior choice of epic weapon.

Appearances

  • Peak has a Scout 14 in Hauling Ash.
  • Some Skeety Kitty Arms locations sell Scout 14s.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Scout 14 if he has level four of Super Strapped.

Trivia

  • The Scout 14 is based on the M14 rifle.
