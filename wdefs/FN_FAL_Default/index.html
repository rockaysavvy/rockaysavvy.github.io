The SLR is a rare assault rifle. It resembles the Slate 36 with hugely improved magazine capacity but worse damage per shot and reload speed, rendering it a substandard selection among assault rifles.

Appearances

  • Some Skeety Kitty Arms locations sell SLRs.
  • Some tier III Graves and Sicarios carry SLRs.
  • In turf attacks and defenses, tier III Sicarios may have SLRs.
  • Some tier III Sicario captains wield SLRs.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with an SLR if he has level three of Super Strapped.

Trivia

  • The SLR is based on the FN FAL.
  • The name “SLR” comes from the L1A1 Self-Loading Rifle, a variant of the FAL.
