The Kellgern 9 Elite S is an epic SMG, a modified Kellgern 9 Elite equipped with a suppressor. Its low damage per shot vitiates the combat effectiveness of this otherwise balanced primary weapon.

Appearances

  • The Spy in Undercover has a Kellgern 9 Elite S.
  • Elite hitmen may join Baker’s crew with Kellgern 9 Elite Ss.
  • In Baker’s Battle, elite thieves and gangsters may also bring Kellgern 9 Elite Ss when Baker hires them.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Kellgern 9 Elite S if he has level two of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Kellgern 9 Elite Ss when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Kellgern 9 Elite Ss to players of any respect level. In multiplayer, players who are at least Respected can buy Kellgern 9 Elite Ss from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Kellgern 9 Elite S by completing a medium or long shuffle in multiplayer.
