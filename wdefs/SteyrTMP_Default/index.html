Sting

$5,700–6,650
Damage90
RPM800
Range1500
Accuracy5.5
Magazine size20

The Sting is an unlockable common submachine gun and Cage’s signature primary weapon, similar to the Kellgern 9. It’s one of the worst submachine guns, because its exceptional reload speed fails to make up for its very poor handling, accuracy, overall damage output, and magazine capacity.

Appearances

  • Cage has a Sting.
  • Tier I Graves, tier I Sicarios, and tier I SWAT officers can wield Stings.
  • In turf attacks and defenses, some tier I Sicarios use Stings.
  • Tier I Sicario captains may have Stings.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Sting if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Stings when they re-take a turf they have previously captured.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect and connected players can purchase Stings from the black market in the campaign.
  • Common generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign may have Stings.

Trivia

  • The Sting is based on the Steyr TMP.
