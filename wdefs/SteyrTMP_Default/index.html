Sting

$5,700–6,650
Damage90
RPM800
Range1400
Accuracy5.5
Magazine size20

The Sting is a common SMG. Combining very low damage with mediocre rate of fire and range, it is one of the weakest primary weapons available to heisters.

Appearances

  • Tier I SWAT officers and Tier I–II Sicarios can carry Stings.
  • Tier I Sicario captains wield Stings.
  • Tier I gangsters that Baker hires may have Stings.
  • In multiplayer, Tier II gangsters and Tier I thieves can also bring Stings to the crew.
  • Low respect and Connected players can purchase Stings from the black market.

Trivia

  • The Sting is based on the Steyr TMP.
