The Practical R is a rare semiautomatic shotgun, a modified Practical equipped with a red dot sight. This increases the weapon’s accuracy and thus its viability in long-range combat. It is one of Dollar Dragon’s signature weapons.

Appearances

  • Dragon has a Practical R when he joins Baker’s crew in multiplayer.
  • Elite thieves may join Baker’s crew with Practical Rs.
  • In Baker’s Battle, veteran thieves and gangsters as well as elite hitmen may also bring Practical Rs when Baker hires them.
  • Vamp can carry a Practical R in New Blood.
  • Some Skeety Kitty Arms locations sell Practical Rs.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Practical R if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Practical Rs when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Practical Rs to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Players who are at least Connected can purchase Practical Rs from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Practical R by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.
