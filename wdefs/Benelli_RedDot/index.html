The Practical R is an unlockable rare shotgun and Dollar Dragon’s signature primary weapon. It comprises a modified Practical with a red dot sight which provides marginally boosted performance, similar to the Urban Striker. It’s an ineffective rare weapon, because its excellent overall damage output fails to compensate for its poor reload speed, magazine capacity, handling, and accuracy.

Appearances

  • Dragon wields a Practical R in the campaign.
  • Some Skeety Kitty Arms locations sell Practical Rs.
  • Players can find Practical Rs in the weapons vaults in compound basements.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Practical R if he has level three of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Practical Rs to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Connected, respected, formidable, and godfather players can also purchase Practical Rs from the black market.
  • Rare generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign may have Practical Rs.
