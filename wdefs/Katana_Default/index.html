The Katana is a legendary melee weapon. It is the strongest melee weapon that heisters can wield, but players cannot purchase it from the black market or the Arms Dealer—they must pick it up during a mission.

Appearances

  • Detective Mark carries a Katana and drops it upon his death.
  • Tier III Rider captains wield Katanas.
  • Ronin have Katanas, including the one in the Dragon Dogs vault.
