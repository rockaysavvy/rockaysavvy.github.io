The Katana is an unlockable uncommon sharp melee weapon and a signature weapon of Midnight and Runaway, in the same class as the Shovel. It’s an extremely strong melee weapon due to its excellent overall damage output.

Appearances

  • Detective Mark, Midnight, and Runaway all wield Katanas.
  • Rōnin have Katanas, including the one in the Dragon Dogs vault.
  • Tier III Rider captains can use Katanas.
  • In character stories, rōnin have Katanas.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Katana if he has level two of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Katanas when they capture a turf.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect, connected, and respected players can purchase Katanas from the black market in the campaign.
  • Uncommon generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign sometimes carry Katanas.
