The Katana is an unlockable uncommon sharp melee weapon and a signature weapon of Midnight and Runaway, in the same class as the Shovel. With its impressive overall damage output, it is the most effective unlockable melee weapon.

Appearances

  • Detective Mark, Midnight, and Runaway all wield Katanas.
  • Rōnin have Katanas, including the one in the Dragon Dogs vault.
  • Tier III Rider captains have Katanas.
  • In story missions, rōnin use Katanas.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Katana if he has level two of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Katanas when they capture a turf or re-take a turf they have previously captured.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect, connected, respected, and formidable players can purchase Katanas from the black market in the campaign.
