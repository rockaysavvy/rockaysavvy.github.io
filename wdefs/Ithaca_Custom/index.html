The Pump Mk. II is a rare pump-action shotgun, a modified Pump with improved damage, range, and accuracy. These enhancements make the Pump Mk. II more viable in medium-range combat. It is one of Mr. X’s signature weapons.

Appearances

  • Mr. X carries a Pump Mk. II when he joins Baker’s crew.
  • Elite thieves may join Baker’s crew with Pump Mk. IIs.
  • In Baker’s Battle, veteran thieves and gangsters as well as elite hitmen may also bring Pump Mk. IIs when Baker hires them.
  • Mr. X may carry a Pump Mk. II in Hauling Ash and The Big Khan.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Pump Mk. II if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Pump Mk. IIs when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Pump Mk. IIs to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Connected or higher players can purchase Pump Mk. IIs from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Pump Mk. II by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.
