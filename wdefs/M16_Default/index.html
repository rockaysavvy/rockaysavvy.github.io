Chopper

$22,000–24,200
Damage130
RPM700
Range2300
Accuracy2.1
Magazine size20

The Chopper is an unlockable rare assault rifle. It’s comparable to the TIMM 4 with improved damage per shot but notably worse magazine capacity, rendering it a respectable choice of rare weapon.

Appearances

  • Jake and Martinez have Choppers.
  • Several park rangers patrolling the mangrove in Trespassing use Choppers.
  • The jet pilot in Jet Set has a Chopper.
  • Some of the Contras in Dish Order use Choppers.
  • Some of the police officers in Break Out carry Choppers.
  • Some Skeety Kitty Arms locations sell Choppers.
  • Cracker wields a Chopper in On Rails.
  • There are several Choppers in the RCPD weapon lockers in Break Out.
  • Ranger has a Chopper when he joins Baker’s crew in the campaign.
  • Ranger carries a Chopper in Pinned Down.
  • Ranger also wields a Chopper in Danger Close.
  • The boss props up Choppers next to his throne at certain levels.
  • Players can find Choppers in the weapons vaults in compound basements.
  • Park rangers always use Choppers, and tier II Chicos, tier II Graves, tier II Cagnali soldiers, tier II Sicarios, elite security guards, police officers, advanced police officers, and tier I SWAT officers can also wield them.
  • In turf attacks and defenses, some tier II Chicos, Cagnali soldiers, and Sicarios have Choppers.
  • Tier II Chico captains and Sicario captains can wield Choppers.
  • In story missions, GI captains, GI lieutenants, GI soldiers, and East Men use Choppers, and Graves, prison guards, and park rangers can also use them.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Chopper if he has level three of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Choppers to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Connected, respected, formidable, and godfather players can also purchase Choppers from the black market.
  • Rare generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign sometimes carry Choppers.

Trivia

  • The Chopper is based on the M16.
