Chopper

Damage130
RPM700
Range2300
Accuracy2.1
Magazine size20

The Chopper is an unlockable rare assault rifle. It is like the TIMM 4 with improved damage per shot but notably worse magazine capacity, rendering it a respectable option among the unlockable rare weapons.

Appearances

  • Jake and Martinez wield Choppers.
  • Some park rangers patrolling the mangrove in Trespassing have Choppers.
  • The jet pilot in Jet Set wields a Chopper.
  • Some of the Contras in Dish Order carry Choppers.
  • Some of the police officers in Break Out have Choppers.
  • Cracker carries a Chopper in On Rails.
  • There are several Choppers in the RCPD weapon lockers in Break Out.
  • Ranger has a Chopper when he joins Baker’s crew in the campaign.
  • Ranger wields a Chopper in Pinned Down.
  • Ranger also uses a Chopper in Danger Close.
  • The boss props up Choppers next to his throne at certain levels.
  • Players can find Choppers in the weapons vaults in compound basements.
  • Park rangers have Choppers, and tier II Chicos, tier II Graves, tier II Scudos, tier II Sicarios, elite security guards, police officers, advanced police officers, and tier I SWAT officers may also use them.
  • In turf attacks and defenses, some tier II Chicos, Scudos, and Sicarios carry Choppers.
  • Tier II Chico and Sicario captains may have Choppers.
  • In story missions, GI captains, GI lieutenants, GI soldiers, and East Men carry Choppers, and Graves, prison guards, and park rangers may also have them.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Chopper if he has level three of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Choppers to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Players who are at least connected can also purchase Choppers from the black market.

Trivia

  • The Chopper is based on the M16.
