The SMG 9mm EGHS is an unlockable legendary submachine gun, comprising a heavily modified SMG 9mm which provides enormously boosted performance, similar to the Vintovka U RS. With its superb overall damage output, reload speed, and controllability, it’s a fantastic weapon.

Appearances

  • Stinger has an SMG 9mm EGHS in Secret Symphony.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with an SMG 9mm EGHS if he has level five of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell SMG 9mm EGHSs to respected, formidable, godfather, and crime boss players in Baker’s Battle.
