The Baseball bat is an unlockable common blunt melee weapon and Jupiter’s signature primary weapon, similar to the Boxer. It’s an ineffective melee weapon, because its respectable range fails to compensate for its atrocious overall damage output and precision.

Appearances

  • Detective Dolph wields a bat.
  • Detective Donny carries a bat, except in Break Out.
  • Jupiter has a bat when she joins Baker’s crew in the campaign. She also carries one in multiplayer.
  • One of the Nemesis’s henchmen uses a bat to torture Cracker in Hook Up.
  • Donny assaults Jupiter with a bat when she refuses to hand over her client list in Battered.
  • There is a bat hanging on the wall in The Blue Bull, which Jupiter can wield against Scorpio and his men.
  • Tier II Sicarios always have bats, and tier I Chicos and Scudos can also use them.
  • In turf attacks and defenses, tier II Chicos and Sicarios carry bats, and some tier I Scudos also wield them.
  • Tier I Rider captains always have bats.
  • In story missions, Chicos, Riders, and Sicarios may carry bats.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a bat if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect and connected players can purchase bats from the black market in the campaign.
  • Generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign sometimes use bats.

Trivia

  • The bat has a Bay Crabs decal on its barrel.
