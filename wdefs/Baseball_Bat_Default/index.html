The Baseball bat is a common blunt melee weapon. It has a slightly longer range than shorter weapons like the Knife and Fists, but its slow speed and low damage undermine its effectiveness.

Appearances

  • Detectives Dolph and Donny carry bats. Donny assaults Jupiter with one when she refuses to hand over her client list in Battered.
  • There is a bat hanging on the wall in The Blue Bull, which Jupiter can wield against Scorpio and his men.
  • One of the Nemesis’s henchmen uses a bat to torture Cracker in Hook Up.
  • Sicarios, Riders, and Chicos, can carry bats in story missions.
  • Tier I–II Chicos, Tier II Sicarios, and Tier I Scudos may wield bats.
  • Tier I Rider captains have bats.
  • Rookie thieves and gangsters may have bats when Baker hires them.
  • Low respect and Connected players can purchase bats from the black market.

Trivia

  • The bat has a Bay Crabs decal on its barrel.
