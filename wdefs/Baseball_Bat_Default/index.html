The Baseball bat is an unlockable common blunt melee weapon and Jupiter’s signature primary weapon, in the same class as the Boxer. It is a weak melee weapon, since its respectable range doesn’t compensate for its terrible overall damage output and precision.

Appearances

  • Detective Dolph carries a bat.
  • Detective Donny wields a bat, except in Break Out.
  • Jupiter has a bat when she joins Baker’s crew in the campaign. She also carries one in multiplayer.
  • One of the Nemesis’s henchmen uses a bat to torture Cracker in Hook Up.
  • Donny assaults Jupiter with a bat when she refuses to hand over her client list in Battered.
  • There is a bat hanging on the wall in The Blue Bull, which Jupiter can wield against Scorpio and his men.
  • Some tier I Chicos, tier I Cagnali soldiers, and tier II Sicarios have bats.
  • In turf attacks and defenses, tier II Chicos, tier I Cagnali soldiers, and tier II Sicarios may use bats.
  • Some tier I Rider captains have bats.
  • In story missions, Chicos, Riders, and Sicarios may use bats.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a bat if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find bats when they re-take a turf they have previously captured.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect and connected players can purchase bats from the black market in the campaign.
  • Common generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign sometimes have bats.

Trivia

  • The bat has a Bay Crabs decal on its barrel.
