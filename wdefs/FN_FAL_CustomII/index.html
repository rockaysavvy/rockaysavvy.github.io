The SLR HG Elite is an epic assault rifle. It consists of a heavily modified SLR with a holo sight and an angled foregrip, which offer significantly boosted performance. It’s like the Slate 36 with hugely improved magazine capacity and overall damage output but worse reload speed, making it a reasonable selection among assault rifles.

Appearances

  • Clutch uses an SLR HG Elite in Who Took the Dogs Out?, and Vamp carries one in New Blood.
  • Detective Patrick uses an SLR HG Elite.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with an SLR HG Elite if he has level four of Super Strapped.
