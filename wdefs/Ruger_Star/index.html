YA-R3K Star

Range1500

Magazine size8

The YA-R3K Star is an epic hand cannon, a modified YA-R3K with improved damage and a pink star-pattern finish. Its balanced accuracy, damage output, and magazine capacity give heisters a versatile secondary weapon choice.

Appearances

  • Elite hitmen may join Baker’s crew with YA-R3K Stars.
  • In Baker’s Battle, elite thieves and gangsters may also bring YA-R3K Stars when Baker hires them.
  • Cracker may carry a YA-R3K Star in Hauling Ash, Jupiter may carry one in King's Treasure, Stinger may carry one in Secret Symphony, and Vamp may carry one in New Blood.
  • The Graves may find YA-R3K Stars when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell YA-R3K Stars to players of any respect level. In multiplayer, players who are at least Respected can buy YA-R3K Stars from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a YA-R3K Star by completing a medium or long shuffle in multiplayer.
