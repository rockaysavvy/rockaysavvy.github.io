Wiz’s Rifle is a unique legendary marksman rifle and Wiz’s signature primary weapon. It is comparable to the SLR with hugely improved damage per shot but hugely worse controllability and overall damage output, making it an inferior choice of legendary weapon.

Appearances

  • Wiz uses this rifle when he joins Baker’s crew in the campaign.
  • Wiz picks up this rifle from his bathtub when he flees the Chicos in Tight Rope and uses it to fend them off in Spend Money To Make Money.
