The Micro SMG ES is a unique epic submachine gun. It consists of a modified Micro SMG with an extended magazine and a suppressor, which offer substantially improved performance. It is similar to the Kellgern 9 Elite with improved accuracy but much worse overall damage output, rendering it an unexceptional choice of weapon.
