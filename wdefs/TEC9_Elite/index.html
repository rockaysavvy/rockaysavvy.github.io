Kellgern 9 Elite

The Kellgern 9 Elite is an unlockable rare submachine gun. It consists of a modified Kellgern 9 with an extended quickdraw magazine which provides significantly boosted performance. It performs similar to the Micro SMG ER with significantly better overall damage output but somewhat worse handling, making it a passable choice of rare weapon.

Appearances

  • Players can find Kellgern 9 Elites in the weapons vaults in compound basements.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Kellgern 9 Elite if he has level three of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Kellgern 9 Elites to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Connected, respected, formidable, and godfather players can also purchase Kellgern 9 Elites from the black market.
  • Rare generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign may use Kellgern 9 Elites.
