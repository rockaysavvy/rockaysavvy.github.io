The Crowbar is an uncommon blunt melee weapon. It has relatively short range but does significant damage per hit.

Appearances

  • Players wield a Crowbar in Street Smarts.
  • Tier I backup and Tier II–III Riders may wield Crowbars.
  • Sicarios and Riders, as well as Chico snipers, can have Crowbars in story missions.
  • Veteran thieves can have Crowbars when Baker hires them.
  • Some Skeety Kitty Arms locations sell Crowbars.
  • The Graves may find Crowbars when they re-take a turf they have previously captured.
  • Low respect, Connected, and Respected players can purchase Crowbars from the black market, as can campaign players who are Formidable.
  • Players can also obtain a Crowbar by completing a short shuffle in multiplayer.
