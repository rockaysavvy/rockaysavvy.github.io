The PDW H is an unlockable epic submachine gun from the Heavy Hitters Pack. It consists of a modified PDW with a holo sight which offers about the same performance. It is similar to the SMG 9mm ERG with much improved magazine capacity and overall damage output but worse reload speed and damage per shot, making it a respectable choice of weapon.

Appearances

  • Players who own the Heavy Hitters Pack automatically unlock the PDW H in multiplayer and receive a PDW H at the beginning of every campaign playthrough.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a PDW H if he has level four of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell PDW Hs to players of any respect level in Baker’s Battle.
