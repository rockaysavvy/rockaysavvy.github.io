The PDW H is a rare SMG from the Heavy Hitters Pack, a modified PDW equipped with a holographic sight. Its improved accuracy makes the PDW H more effective at medium-to-long ranges. It is functionally identical to the PDW R.

Appearances

  • Elite thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with PDW Hs.
  • In Baker’s Battle, veteran thieves and gangsters as well as elite hitmen may also bring PDW Hs when Baker hires them.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a PDW H if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find PDW Hs when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell PDW Hs to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Players who are at least Connected can purchase PDW Hs from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a PDW H by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.
