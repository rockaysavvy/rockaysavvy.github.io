Jupiter's SPX is Jupiter’s signature modification of the SPX: a unique epic shotgun equipped with a holographic sight.

Appearances

  • Jupiter picks up this shotgun from the bag her father gives her in Jupiter's Gym. She has it with her in Steroid Fire and can use it in other missions after killing Scorpio. The police confiscate it in Break Out, but she picks it back up from the weapons locker.
  • Jupiter also has the shotgun when players unlock her in multiplayer.
  • Jupiter may carry this shotgun in King's Treasure and Juiced Up, Iced Out.
