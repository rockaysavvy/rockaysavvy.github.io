Jupiter’s SPX

Damage1380
RPM85

Accuracy5.4
Magazine size7

Jupiter’s SPX is a unique epic shotgun. It consists of a modified SPX with a holo sight which offers marginally boosted performance, in the same class as the Pump. It is among the weakest epic weapons, since its superior damage per shot doesn’t compensate for its awful controllability, accuracy, magazine capacity, and reload speed.

Appearances

  • Jupiter wields this shotgun in King’s Treasure and Juiced Up, Iced Out.
  • In the campaign, Jupiter picks up this SPX from the bag her father gives her in Jupiter’s Gym. After she kills Scorpio in Steroid Fire, she modifies it into its final form and can use it in other missions.
  • The police confiscate the the shotgun in Break Out, but Jupiter picks it back up from the weapons locker.
