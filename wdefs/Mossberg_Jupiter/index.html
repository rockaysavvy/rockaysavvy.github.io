Jupiter’s SPX

Damage1500
RPM110

Accuracy4.9
Magazine size7

Jupiter’s SPX is a unique epic shotgun. It consists of a modified SPX with a holo sight which offers slightly improved performance, similar to the Practical. It’s a subpar epic weapon, since its impressive overall damage output fails to make up for its atrocious controllability, accuracy, magazine capacity, and reload speed.

Appearances

  • Jupiter has this shotgun in King’s Treasure and Juiced Up, Iced Out.
  • In the campaign, Jupiter picks up this SPX from the bag her father gives her in Jupiter’s Gym. After she kills Scorpio in Steroid Fire, she modifies it into its final form and can use it in other missions.
  • The police confiscate the the shotgun in Break Out, but Jupiter picks it back up from the weapons locker.
