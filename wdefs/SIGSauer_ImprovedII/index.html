Magazine size10

The Swiss E+ is an unlockable uncommon pistol and Tuna’s signature secondary weapon. It comprises a modified Swiss with an extended quickdraw magazine which provides marginally boosted performance. It’s comparable to the FDE Tactical with somewhat improved damage per shot but much worse overall damage output and controllability, making it a decent choice of pistol.

Appearances

  • Stinger has a Swiss E+ in Silence is Gold, and Tulip wields one in The Big Khan.
  • Tuna uses a Swiss E+ in campaign and multiplayer.
  • The Graves may find Swiss E+s when they capture a turf or re-take a turf they have previously captured.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect, connected, respected, and formidable players can purchase Swiss E+s from the black market in the campaign.
