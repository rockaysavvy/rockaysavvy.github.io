Swiss E+

$3,850–4,620
Damage159
RPM600
Range1000
Accuracy2.5
Magazine size10

The Swiss E+ is an unlockable uncommon pistol and Tuna’s signature secondary weapon. It comprises a modified Swiss with an extended quickdraw magazine which offers marginally boosted performance. It is comparable to the FDE Tactical with slightly improved damage per shot but notably worse overall damage output and handling, rendering it an unexceptional option among the unlockable weapons.

Appearances

  • Stinger carries a Swiss E+ in Silence is Gold, and Tulip has one in The Big Khan.
  • Tuna uses a Swiss E+ in campaign and multiplayer.
  • The Graves may find Swiss E+s when they capture a turf.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect, connected, and respected players can purchase Swiss E+s from the black market in the campaign.
  • Uncommon generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign sometimes use Swiss E+s.
