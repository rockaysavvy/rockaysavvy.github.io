Swiss Elite ES

$14,700–21,000
Damage155
RPM600
Range1200
Accuracy2.3
Magazine size13

The Swiss Elite ES is an epic semiautomatic pistol, a heavily modified Swiss with significantly improved performance. It is one of Tulip’s signature weapons.

Appearances

  • Tulip brings a Swiss Elite ES with him Baker hires him.
  • Elite hitmen may join Baker’s crew with Swiss Elite ESs.
  • In Baker’s Battle, elite thieves and gangsters may also bring Swiss Elite ESs when Baker hires them.
  • Tulip may carry a Swiss Elite ES in Secret Symphony and The Big Khan, and Stinger may carry one in Silence is Gold.
  • The Graves may find Swiss Elite ESs when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Swiss Elite ESs to players of any respect level. In multiplayer, players who are at least Respected can buy Swiss Elite ESs from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Swiss Elite ES by completing a medium or long shuffle in multiplayer.
