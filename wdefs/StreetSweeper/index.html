Urban Striker

RPM180
Range900
Accuracy7.0
Magazine size8

The Urban Striker is a rare revolving shotgun from the Tactical Weapon Pack. Its high damage output keeps it viable for close-range combat despite its laborious reloading process.

Appearances

  • Players who own the Tactical Weapon Pack receive an Urban Striker at the beginning of each campaign playthrough.
  • Elite thieves may join Baker’s crew with Urban Strikers.
  • In Baker’s Battle, veteran thieves and gangsters as well as elite hitmen may also bring Urban Strikers when Baker hires them.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with an Urban Striker if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Urban Strikers when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Urban Strikers to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Players who are at least Connected can purchase Urban Strikers from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain an Urban Striker by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.

Trivia

  • The Urban Striker is loosely based on the Armsel Striker and the SWD Street Sweeper.
