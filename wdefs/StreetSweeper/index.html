Urban Striker

$26,300–37,500
Damage1260
RPM160
Range1000
Accuracy8.0
Magazine size8

The Urban Striker is an unlockable epic shotgun from the Tactical Weapon Pack and Tuna’s signature primary weapon, comparable to the Practical. Even with its exceptional overall damage output, its atrocious accuracy, handling, magazine capacity, and reload speed render it one of the worst epic weapons.

Appearances

  • Players who own the Tactical Weapon Pack automatically unlock the Urban Striker in multiplayer and receive an Urban Striker at the beginning of every campaign playthrough.
  • Tuna wields an Urban Striker in campaign and multiplayer.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with an Urban Striker if he has level four of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Urban Strikers to players of any respect level in Baker’s Battle.

Trivia

  • The Urban Striker is loosely based on the Armsel Striker and the SWD Street Sweeper.
