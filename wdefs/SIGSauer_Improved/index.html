Swiss Elite ES

$8,400–9,240
Damage170
RPM600
Range1125
Accuracy2.2
Magazine size15

The Swiss Elite ES is an unlockable rare pistol and Ranger’s signature secondary weapon. It consists of a heavily modified Swiss with a suppressor and an extended quickdraw magazine, which provide substantially improved performance in the same class as the Brescia 92 ER. Notwithstanding its below-average handling, its superior reload speed renders it among the strongest rare weapons.

Appearances

  • Ranger has a Swiss Elite ES when he joins Baker’s crew in the campaign. He also carries one in multiplayer.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Swiss Elite ESs to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Connected, respected, formidable, and godfather players can also purchase Swiss Elite ESs from the black market.
  • Rare generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign may use Swiss Elite ESs.
