Swiss E

$3,850–4,620
Damage155
RPM600
Range1000
Accuracy2.6
Magazine size12

The Swiss E is an uncommon modified Swiss with an extended magazine and increased damage. These enhancements make the pistol significantly more viable as a sidearm.

Appearances

  • Veteran thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with a Swiss E.
  • In multiplayer, elite thieves and gangsters may also have Swiss Es when Baker hires them.
  • The Graves may find Swiss Es when they re-take a turf they have previously captured.
  • Low respect, Connected, and Respected players can purchase Swiss Es from the black market, as can campaign players who are Formidable.
  • Players can also obtain a Swiss E by completing a short shuffle in multiplayer.
