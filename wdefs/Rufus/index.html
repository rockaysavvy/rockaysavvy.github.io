Rufus

$15,000–16,500
Damage1200
RPM120
Range1100
Accuracy8.0
Magazine size2

The Rufus is a rare double-barrel shotgun from the Heavy Hitters Pack. It only fires 12 pellets compared to the Rufus short’s 20, so it does much less damage and is a rather ineffective primary weapon.

Appearances

  • Players who own the Heavy Hitters Pack receive a Rufus at the beginning of each campaign playthrough.
  • Elite thieves may join Baker’s crew with Rufuses.
  • In Baker’s Battle, veteran thieves and gangsters as well as elite hitmen may also bring Rufuses when Baker hires them.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Rufus if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Rufuses when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Rufuses to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Players who are at least Connected can purchase Rufuses from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Rufus by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.

Trivia

  • The Rufus is based on the Rossi Overland.
