Rufus

Range900
Accuracy9.0
Magazine size2

The Rufus is an unlockable rare shotgun from the Heavy Hitters Pack and Ropes’s signature primary weapon. It is comparable to the Practical with enormously better reload speed but immensely worse controllability, accuracy, and magazine capacity, rendering it an inferior choice among the unlockable weapons.

Appearances

  • Players who own the Heavy Hitters Pack automatically unlock the Rufus in multiplayer and receive a Rufus at the beginning of every campaign playthrough.
  • Ropes carries a Rufus.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Rufus if he has level three of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Rufuses to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Players who are at least connected can also purchase Rufuses from the black market.

Trivia

  • The Rufus is based on the Rossi Overland.
