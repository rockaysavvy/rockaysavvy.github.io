The Machete is an unlockable common melee weapon and Macho’s signature primary weapon. It behaves similar to the Shovel but with worse range and overall damage output, making it a respectable choice of melee weapon.

Appearances

  • Chase used to have a Machete as his default melee weapon, but this was removed.
  • Macho has a Machete.
  • Some of Scorpio’s thugs carry Machetes when they confront Jupiter at The Blue Bull.
  • Tier III Sicarios always wield Machetes, and tier II–III Graves can also wield them.
  • In turf attacks and defenses, tier III Sicarios have Machetes.
  • Tier II Rider captains always carry Machetes.
  • In character stories, Witch goons have Machetes, and Chicos, Scudos, and Sicarios may also have them.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Machete if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect and connected players can purchase Machetes from the black market in the campaign.
  • Generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign may have Machetes.
