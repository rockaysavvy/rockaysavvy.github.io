The YA-R3K is a rare hand cannon. Its larger cylinder and quick reload speed give it formidable damage output even compared to higher-quality sidearms. It is a signature weapon of both Chase and Ranger.

Appearances

  • Chase and Ranger have YA-R3Ks when Baker hires them. Chase also has a YA-R3K in The Predator.
  • Whitey wields a YA-R3K, as does Detective Donny in Break Out.
  • Touchdown defends turfs with a YA-R3K after Baker upgrades his army to Tier III.
  • Tier III backup and Tier III SWAT officers also carry YA-R3Ks.
  • Elite thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with YA-R3Ks.
  • In Baker’s Battle, veteran thieves and gangsters as well as elite hitmen may also bring YA-R3Ks when Baker hires them.
  • Ropes may carry a YA-R3K in New Blood, Chase may carry one in New Blood, and Ranger may carry one in King's Treasure and Secret Symphony.
  • The Graves may find YA-R3Ks when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell YA-R3Ks to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Players who are at least Connected can purchase YA-R3Ks from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a YA-R3K by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.
