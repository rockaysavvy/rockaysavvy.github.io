YA-R3K

$11,000–12,100
Damage350
RPM300
Range1500
Accuracy1.5
Magazine size8

The YA-R3K is an unlockable rare hand cannon and Tank’s signature secondary weapon. It behaves like the 357 BOA with much improved magazine capacity and long-range damage output but worse damage per shot and handling, making it a substandard selection among the unlockable rare weapons.

Appearances

  • Chase wields a YA-R3K in New Blood, Ranger carries one in King’s Treasure and Secret Symphony, and Theresa has one in Cagnali’s Order.
  • Tank and Whitey carry YA-R3Ks.
  • Detective Donny uses a YA-R3K in Break Out.
  • Chase wields a YA-R3K in The Predator.
  • Players can find YA-R3Ks in the weapons vaults in compound basements.
  • Tier III Graves and SWAT officers can wield YA-R3Ks.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell YA-R3Ks to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Connected, respected, formidable, and godfather players can also purchase YA-R3Ks from the black market.
  • Rare generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign may use YA-R3Ks.

Trivia

  • The YA-R3K is named after Jarek Kolář, the game’s Development Director.
