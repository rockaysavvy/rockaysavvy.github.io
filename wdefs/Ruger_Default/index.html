YA-R3K

$11,000–12,100
Damage350
RPM300
Range1500
Accuracy1.5
Magazine size8

The YA-R3K is an unlockable rare hand cannon and Tank’s signature secondary weapon. It is like the Bearslayer with significantly better magazine capacity but marginally worse accuracy and overall damage output, rendering it an acceptable choice of secondary weapon.

Appearances

  • Chase wields a YA-R3K in New Blood, and Ranger carries one in King’s Treasure and Secret Symphony.
  • Tank and Whitey wield YA-R3Ks.
  • Detective Donny carries a YA-R3K in Break Out.
  • Chase has a YA-R3K in The Predator.
  • Players can find YA-R3Ks in the weapons vaults in compound basements.
  • Tier III SWAT officers carry YA-R3Ks, and tier III Graves may also carry them.
  • Touchdown has a YA-R3K when Baker’s army is tier III.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell YA-R3Ks to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Connected or higher players can also purchase YA-R3Ks from the black market.
