BU-RP E (SWAT)

Damage135
RPM1100
Range1700
Accuracy3.4
Magazine size30

The SWAT BU-RP E is a unique epic submachine gun. It’s comparable to the Scorp with better overall damage output and accuracy but worse reload speed and magazine capacity, making it a respectable choice among submachine guns. Contrary to its name, it does not have an extended magazine.

Appearances

  • The SWAT officers and Viet Cong soldiers in Operation Red Star can have BU-RP Es.
  • Advanced police officers and tier II SWAT officers may use BU-RP Es.
  • In story missions, police officers may carry BU-RP Es.
