Kellgern 9

$9,500–11,400
Damage80
RPM1000
Range1500
Accuracy5.0
Magazine size24

The Kellgern 9 is an unlockable uncommon submachine gun. It behaves similar to the Micro SMG with better damage per shot but worse magazine capacity and accuracy, making it an adequate selection among the unlockable uncommon weapons.

Appearances

  • The Gemini has a Kellgern 9.
  • Detective Phips uses a Kellgern 9 in story missions.
  • Baker’s crew gives Khan’s dealer a Kellgern 9 in Rescue Khan’s Dealer.
  • Several of the Scudos working with the Nemesis in Hook Up have Kellgern 9s.
  • Some of the Chicos in Tight Rope carry Kellgern 9s.
  • Rōnin have Kellgern 9s, including the one in the Dragon Dogs vault.
  • Some tier I Chicos and Cagnali soldiers use Kellgern 9s.
  • In turf attacks and defenses, tier I Chicos and Cagnali soldiers may have Kellgern 9s.
  • Some tier I Chico captains and Scudo captains use Kellgern 9s.
  • In story missions, rōnin carry Kellgern 9s, and Chicos may also use them.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Kellgern 9 if he has level two of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Kellgern 9s when they capture a turf.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect, connected, and respected players can purchase Kellgern 9s from the black market in the campaign.
  • Uncommon generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign may use Kellgern 9s.

Trivia

  • The Kellgern 9 is based on the Intratec TEC-9.
  • “Kellgern” is a reference to George Kellgren, who designed the TEC-9.
