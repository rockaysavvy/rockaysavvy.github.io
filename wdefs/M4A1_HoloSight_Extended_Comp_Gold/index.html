The TIMM 4 EHMB is a legendary assault rifle, a heavily modified TIMM 4 with a flashy gold finish. Its high accuracy and controllability compensate for its less-than-stellar damage output. It is one of Lector’s signature weapons.

Appearances

  • Lector has a TIMM 4 EHMB when Baker hires him.
  • Baker used to carry a TIMM 4 EHMB in Under Siege.
  • Tulip may carry a TIMM 4 EHMB in The Big Khan, and Lector may carry one in Who Took the Dogs Out?.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a TIMM 4 EHMB if he has level three of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find TIMM 4 EHMBs when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell TIMM 4 EHMBs to Respected or higher players. In multiplayer, players who are at least Formidable can buy TIMM 4 EHMBs from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a TIMM 4 EHMB by completing a long shuffle in multiplayer.
