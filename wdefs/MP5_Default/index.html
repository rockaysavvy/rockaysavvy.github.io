Magazine size30

The SMG 9mm is an epic SMG. Its limited range and low damage output render it ineffective outside of close combat.

Appearances

  • CIA agents carry SMG 9mms, as does the Nemesis in Hook Up.
  • Police officers can wield SMG 9mms.
  • Tier II–III Scudos and Tier III backup may carry SMG 9mms.
  • Tier II Scudo captains have SMG 9mms.
  • Some Chicos have SMG 9mms in story missions.
  • Elite hitmen may join Baker’s crew with SMG 9mms.
  • In Baker’s Battle, elite thieves and gangsters may also bring SMG 9mms when Baker hires them.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with an SMG 9mm if he has level two of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find SMG 9mms when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell SMG 9mms to players of any respect level. In multiplayer, players who are at least Respected can buy SMG 9mms from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain an SMG 9mm by completing a medium or long shuffle in multiplayer.

Trivia

  • The SMG 9mm is based on the Heckler & Koch MP5.
