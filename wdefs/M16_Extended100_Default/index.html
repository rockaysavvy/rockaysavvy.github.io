The Chopper E is an epic burst assault rifle, a modified Chopper equipped with a large drum magazine. The extended capacity helps heisters survive long combat stretches without reloading.

Appearances

  • Elite hitmen may join Baker’s crew with Chopper Es.
  • In Baker’s Battle, elite thieves and gangsters may also bring Chopper Es when Baker hires them.
  • Chase may carry a Chopper E in New Blood, Jupiter may carry one in King's Treasure, and Silver may carry one in The Big Khan.
  • The weapon locker in Gold Cup contains a Chopper E.
  • Some Skeety Kitty Arms locations sell Chopper Es.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Chopper E if he has level two of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Chopper Es when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Chopper Es to players of any respect level. In multiplayer, players who are at least Respected can buy Chopper Es from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Chopper E by completing a medium or long shuffle in multiplayer.
