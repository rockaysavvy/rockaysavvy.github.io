The SAW Para O is an unlockable epic machine gun from the Heavy Hitters Pack. It comprises a modified Reaper’s Saw with a 3x scope which provides marginally boosted performance, in the same class as the Machinegun. It’s the best unlockable machine gun, because its exceptional accuracy and handling make up for its inferior overall damage output.

Appearances

  • Players who own the Heavy Hitters Pack automatically unlock the SAW Para O in multiplayer and can purchase an SAW Para O at the beginning of every campaign playthrough.
  • Players can find SAW Para Os in the weapons vaults in compound basements.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with an SAW Para O if he has level four of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell SAW Para Os to players of any respect level in Baker’s Battle. Respected, formidable, godfather, and crime boss players can also purchase SAW Para Os from the black market.
