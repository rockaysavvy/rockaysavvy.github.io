The Minigun is a unique reserved special weapon, similar to the SAW Para. It is a very strong special weapon due to its exceptional overall damage output and magazine capacity.

Appearances

  • Cagnali uses a Minigun when she defends her last turf and when Baker’s crew confronts her in Cagnali’s Fortress.
  • Tier I minigunners wield Miniguns.
