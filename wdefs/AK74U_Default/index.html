Vintovka U

$22,000–24,200
Damage117
RPM730
Range2500
Accuracy2.2
Magazine size30

The Vintovka U is an unlockable rare assault rifle. It is similar to the SAM-AF1 R with better overall damage output but worse magazine capacity, rendering it a balanced choice of assault rifle.

Appearances

  • Ropes wields a Vintovka U in New Blood and Juiced Up, Iced Out.
  • Lafoushini, Manolo, and Whitey all carry Vintovka Us.
  • Mozart uses a Vintovka U in Freeing Mozart.
  • Some of Scorpio’s thugs have Vintovka Us when they confront Jupiter at The Blue Bull.
  • Some of the Chicos pursuing Wiz in Spend Money To Make Money use Vintovka Us.
  • The weapon locker in Gold Cup contains a Vintovka U.
  • Some tier III Graves and Riders carry Vintovka Us.
  • In turf attacks and defenses, tier III Riders may carry Vintovka Us.
  • Some tier III Scudo captains have Vintovka Us.
  • In story missions, Contras, Russian soldiers, Spetsnaz, Viet Cong snipers, Viet Cong soldiers, and terrorists wield Vintovka Us, and Chicos and Graves can also wield them.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Vintovka U if he has level three of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Vintovka Us to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Connected, respected, formidable, and godfather players can also purchase Vintovka Us from the black market.
  • Rare generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign may have Vintovka Us.

Trivia

  • The Vintovka U is based on the AKS-74U.
