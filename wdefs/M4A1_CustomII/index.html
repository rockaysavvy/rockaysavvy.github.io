The TIMM 4 EH is an unlockable epic assault rifle. It consists of a modified TIMM 4 with an extended quickdraw magazine and a holo sight, which provide marginally boosted performance. It’s similar to the Vintovka U with much improved magazine capacity and overall damage output but slightly worse damage per shot, making it an unexceptional choice among the unlockable epic weapons.

Appearances

  • Players can find TIMM 4 EHs in the weapons vaults in compound basements.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a TIMM 4 EH if he has level four of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell TIMM 4 EHs to players of any respect level in Baker’s Battle.
