The TIMM 4 EHS is a legendary assault rifle, a suppressed and heavily modified TIMM 4. Its accuracy and magazine capacity make stealth eliminations a breeze, and it does enough damage per second to keep heisters safe if things get messy.

Appearances

  • Baker may begin his campaign with a TIMM 4 EHS if he has level three of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find TIMM 4 EHSs when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell TIMM 4 EHSs to Respected or higher players. In multiplayer, Formidable or higher players can buy TIMM 4 EHSs from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a TIMM 4 EHS by completing a long shuffle in multiplayer.
