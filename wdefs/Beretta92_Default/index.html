The Brescia 92 (brɛʃə) is a rare semiautomatic pistol. Its balanced handling, accuracy, and damage output make it an excellent choice of secondary weapon, especially for heisters without access to epic and legendary sidearms.

Appearances

  • Detectives Mark, Jacobs, Brown, Rico, Dolph, Krueger, and Patrick all carry Brescia 92s.
  • Cracker grabs a Brescia 92 off of the table to save himself from the Nemesis’s henchmen in Hook Up. He also carries one in On Rails.
  • Wiz picks up a Brescia 92 from his desk in Tight Rope.
  • Ranger wields a Brescia 92 in Operation Red Star.
  • Players have a Brescia 92 in Street Smarts.
  • Tier I–III Sicarios may carry Brescia 92s; Tier I–III Sicario captains also have Brescia 92s.
  • Security guards as well as the chief guard can carry Brescia 92s.
  • Elite thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with Brescia 92s.
  • In Baker’s Battle, veteran thieves and gangsters as well as elite hitmen may also bring Brescia 92s when Baker hires them.
  • The Graves may find Brescia 92s when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Brescia 92s to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Players who are at least Connected can purchase Brescia 92s from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Brescia 92 by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.

Trivia

  • The Brescia 92 is based on the Beretta 92 FS.
  • Brescia is the Italian province where Beretta is headquartered.
