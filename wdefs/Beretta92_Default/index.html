Brescia 92

$3,500–4,200
Damage165
RPM522
Range1000
Accuracy2.2
Magazine size15

The Brescia 92 is an unlockable uncommon pistol and a signature weapon of Clutch, Peak, and Silver, in the same class as the Swiss Elite ES. It is an effective uncommon weapon because of its exceptional accuracy, overall damage output, and reload speed.

Appearances

  • Detectives Brown, Dolph, Jacobs, Krueger, Mark, Patrick, and Rico wield Brescia 92s.
  • Clutch, Peak, and Silver all use Brescia 92s.
  • Some Scudos working with the Nemesis in Hook Up carry Brescia 92s.
  • Cracker grabs a Brescia 92 off of the table to save himself from the Nemesis’s henchmen in Hook Up.
  • Cracker also wields a Brescia 92 in On Rails.
  • Ranger has a Brescia 92 in Operation Red Star.
  • Wiz picks up a Brescia 92 from his desk in Tight Rope.
  • Some tier I Sicarios, chief guards, and security guards wield Brescia 92s.
  • In turf wars, tier I Sicarios can use Brescia 92s.
  • Tier I Sicario captains always carry Brescia 92s.
  • In character stories, Spetsnaz have Brescia 92s, and Chicos and Riders may also use them.
  • The Graves may find Brescia 92s when they capture a turf.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect, connected, and respected players can purchase Brescia 92s from the black market in the campaign.
  • Uncommon generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign may use Brescia 92s.

Trivia

  • The Brescia 92 is based on the Beretta 92 FS.
  • Brescia is the Italian province where Beretta is headquartered.
