The Sturm GWR Gold is a legendary burst assault rifle, a Sturm GWR with an intricate gold finish. It is one of Tulip’s signature weapons and is functionally identical to the Sturm GWR.

Appearances

  • Tulip has a Sturm GWR Gold when he joins Baker’s crew.
  • Tulip may carry a Sturm GWR Gold in Secret Symphony and The Big Khan.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Sturm GWR Gold if he has level three of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Sturm GWR Golds when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Sturm GWR Golds to Respected or higher players. In multiplayer, players who are at least Formidable can buy Sturm GWR Golds from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Sturm GWR Gold by completing a long shuffle in multiplayer.
