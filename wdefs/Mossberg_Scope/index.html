The SPX O is an unlockable legendary shotgun. It consists of a modified SPX with a 3x scope which provides substantially improved performance, comparable to the Pump Mk. II. Its superior damage per shot fails to compensate for its awful controllability, accuracy, and reload speed, rendering it one of the worst legendary weapons.

Appearances

  • Baker may begin his campaign with an SPX O if he has level five of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell SPX Os to respected, formidable, godfather, and crime boss players in Baker’s Battle.
