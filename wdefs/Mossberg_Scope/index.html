The SPX O is an unlockable legendary shotgun. It comprises a modified SPX with a 3x scope which provides substantially improved performance. It is similar to the Practical with notably better damage per shot and accuracy but significantly worse handling and close-range damage output, rendering it an inferior choice among the unlockable weapons.

Appearances

  • Baker may begin his campaign with an SPX O if he has level five of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell SPX Os to respected, formidable, godfather, and crime boss players in Baker’s Battle. Respected, formidable, godfather, and crime boss players can also purchase SPX Os from the black market.
