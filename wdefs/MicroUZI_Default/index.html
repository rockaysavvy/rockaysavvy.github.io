Micro SMG

$9,500–11,400
Damage65
RPM1250
Range1500
Accuracy4.6
Magazine size32

The Micro SMG is an uncommon SMG. Its high rate of fire compensates somewhat for its exceptionally low damage, but it remains one of the weakest primary weapons available to heisters.

Appearances

  • Tier II–III Chicos and Chico captains carry Micro SMGs.
  • Veteran thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with a Micro SMG.
  • In multiplayer, elite thieves and gangsters may also have Micro SMGs when Baker hires them.
  • The Graves may find Micro SMGs when they re-take a turf they have previously captured.
  • Low respect, Connected, and Respected players can purchase Micro SMGs from the black market, as can campaign players who are Formidable.
  • Players can also obtain a Micro SMG by completing a short shuffle in multiplayer.

Trivia

  • The Micro SMG is based on the Micro Uzi.
