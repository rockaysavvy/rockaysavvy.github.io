12 Gauge

$7,500–9,000
Damage750
RPM120
Range900
Accuracy7.0
Magazine size5

The 12 Gauge is an uncommon pump-action shotgun. Due to its small magazine, slow rate of fire, and low damage for a shotgun, it suffices only for small-time heisters.

Appearances

  • Veteran thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with a 12 Gauge.
  • In multiplayer, elite thieves may also have 12 Gauges when Baker hires them.
  • The Graves may find 12 Gauges when they re-take a turf they have previously captured.
  • Low respect, Connected, and Respected players can purchase 12 Gauges from the black market, as can campaign players who are Formidable.
  • Players can also obtain a 12 Gauge by completing a short shuffle in multiplayer.

Trivia

  • The 12 Gauge is based on the Franchi SPAS-12.
