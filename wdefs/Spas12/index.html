12 Gauge

$7,500–9,000
Damage750
RPM130
Range900
Accuracy7.0
Magazine size5

The 12 Gauge is an unlockable uncommon shotgun. It behaves like the Lupara Mk. II with immensely better handling but worse damage per shot, rendering it an inferior choice of weapon.

Appearances

  • Some tier II Graves use 12 Gauges.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a 12 Gauge if he has level two of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find 12 Gauges when they capture a turf.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect, connected, and respected players can purchase 12 Gauges from the black market in the campaign.
  • Uncommon generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign may use 12 Gauges.

Trivia

  • The 12 Gauge is based on the Franchi SPAS-12.
