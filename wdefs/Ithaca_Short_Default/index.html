“Woo-ha! Shotgun!”

The Lupara Short is an unlockable common pump-action shotgun, comparable to the Pump. Despite its respectable damage per shot, its atrocious handling, accuracy, overall damage output, and magazine capacity impair its utility among weapons.

Appearances

  • One of Scorpio’s henchmen brings a Lupara Short when he confronts Jupiter at The Blue Bull.
  • There is a Lupara Short behind the bar at The Blue Bull, which players can pick up during Jupiter’s second story mission.
  • Some tier I Graves and Riders wield Lupara Shorts.
  • In turf attacks and defenses, tier II Cagnali soldiers and tier I Riders can wield Lupara Shorts.
  • Some tier I Rider captains and tier III Scudo captains use Lupara Shorts.
  • In story missions, Graves can have Lupara Shorts.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Lupara Short if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Lupara Shorts when they re-take a turf they have previously captured.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect and connected players can purchase Lupara Shorts from the black market in the campaign.
  • Common generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign may wield Lupara Shorts.

Trivia

  • The Lupara Short is based on the Ithaca 37 Stakeout.
  • “Lupara” means “sawed-off shotgun” in Italian.
