The Lupara Short is an unlockable common pump-action shotgun, in the same class as the Pump. It is a weak weapon, since its first-rate damage per shot fails to make up for its abysmal handling, accuracy, overall damage output, and magazine capacity.

Appearances

  • Saul brings a Lupara Short when he and Scorpio confront Jupiter at The Blue Bull.
  • There is a Lupara Short behind the bar at The Blue Bull, which players can pick up during Jupiter’s second story mission.
  • Tier I Graves and Riders may have Lupara Shorts.
  • In turf wars, some tier I Riders and tier II Scudos use Lupara Shorts.
  • Tier I Rider and tier III Scudo captains may have Lupara Shorts.
  • In story missions, Graves may use Lupara Shorts.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Lupara Short if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect and connected players can purchase Lupara Shorts from the black market in the campaign.
  • Generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign may use Lupara Shorts.

Trivia

  • The Lupara Short is based on the Ithaca 37 Stakeout.
  • “Lupara” means “sawed-off shotgun” in Italian.
