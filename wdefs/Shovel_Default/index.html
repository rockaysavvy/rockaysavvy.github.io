The Shovel is an unlockable common melee weapon and Axe’s signature primary weapon. It performs similar to the Machete but with improved range and overall damage output, rendering it a respectable choice of melee weapon.

Appearances

  • Axe uses a Shovel.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Shovel if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect and connected players can purchase Shovels from the black market in the campaign.
  • Generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign may use Shovels.
