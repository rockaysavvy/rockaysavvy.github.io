Bohema

Damage85
RPM850
Range1000
Accuracy3.8
Magazine size25

The Bohema is an unlockable rare pistol from the Tactical Weapon Pack and Ropes’s signature secondary weapon. It resembles the Glizzy ES with better accuracy but worse overall damage output, rendering it a substandard option among the unlockable rare weapons.

Appearances

  • Players who own the Tactical Weapon Pack automatically unlock the Bohema in multiplayer and receive a Bohema at the beginning of every campaign playthrough.
  • Ropes carries a Bohema.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Bohemas to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Connected or higher players can also purchase Bohemas from the black market.

Trivia

  • The Bohema is based on the CZ 75 Automatic.
