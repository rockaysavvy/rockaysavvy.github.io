The Glizzy ER+ is a rare automatic pistol, a heavily modified Glizzy with a red dot sight, a 28-round quick-draw magazine, boosted damage, and a custom pink palm tree finish. It is a signature weapon of both Vamp and Silver.

Appearances

  • Vamp and Silver have Glizzy ER+s equipped when they join Baker’s crew.
  • Elite thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with Glizzy ER+s.
  • In Baker’s Battle, veteran thieves and gangsters as well as elite hitmen may also bring Glizzy ER+s when Baker hires them.
  • Theresa may carry a Glizzy ER+ in Who Took the Dogs Out?, Vamp may carry one in New Blood, and Silver may carry one in Silence is Gold, The Big Khan, and Who Took the Dogs Out?.
  • The Graves may find Glizzy ER+s when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Glizzy ER+s to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Players who are at least Connected can purchase Glizzy ER+s from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Glizzy ER+ by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.
