Neshek

$22,000–24,200
Damage85
RPM920
Range2000
Accuracy2.0
Magazine size25

The Neshek is a rare assault rifle. It is like the SAM-AF1 with marginally better accuracy, handling, and close-range damage output but worse damage per shot and reload speed, rendering it a respectable choice of rare weapon.

Appearances

  • Some Skeety Kitty Arms locations sell Nesheks.
  • Some tier III Sicarios wield Nesheks.
  • Tier III Sicario captains can use Nesheks.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Neshek if he has level three of Super Strapped.

Known issues

  • The iron sights on the Neshek are improperly aligned, and the point of impact is several pixels above them.
