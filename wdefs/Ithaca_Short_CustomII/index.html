The Lupara Mk. III is a rare further modified Lupara Mk. II with improved spread, reload speed, and damage. It performs acceptably in close combat, but becomes near-useless at range.

Appearances

  • Jupiter has a Lupara Mk. III when she joins Baker’s crew.
  • Elite thieves may join Baker’s crew with Lupara Mk. IIIs.
  • In Baker’s Battle, veteran thieves and gangsters as well as elite hitmen may also bring Lupara Mk. IIIs when Baker hires them.
  • Ropes can carry a Lupara Mk. III in New Blood.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Lupara Mk. III if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Lupara Mk. IIIs when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Lupara Mk. IIIs to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Players who are at least Connected can purchase Lupara Mk. IIIs from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Lupara Mk. III by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.
