The Lupara Mk. III is an unlockable rare shotgun, consisting of a modified Lupara Short which offers substantially improved performance, in the same class as the Pump Mk. II. Despite its respectable damage per shot, its atrocious handling, overall damage output, reload speed, and accuracy make it one of the worst rare weapons.

Appearances

  • Mr. X has a Lupara Mk. III in Hauling Ash and The Big Khan.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Lupara Mk. III if he has level three of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Lupara Mk. IIIs to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Connected or higher players can also purchase Lupara Mk. IIIs from the black market.
