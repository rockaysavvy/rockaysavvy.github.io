Hand Cannon

$19,300–27,500
Damage275
RPM330
Range1500
Accuracy2.0
Magazine size20

The Hand Cannon is an unlockable epic hand cannon and Jupiter’s signature secondary weapon, similar to the Bohema. It is an incredibly effective hand cannon, because its excellent overall damage output, magazine capacity, and handling make up for its inferior accuracy and damage per shot.

Appearances

  • Jupiter has a Hand Cannon in campaign and multiplayer.
  • Players can find Hand Cannons in the weapons vaults in compound basements.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Hand Cannons to players of any respect level in Baker’s Battle. Respected, formidable, godfather, and crime boss players can also purchase Hand Cannons from the black market.
