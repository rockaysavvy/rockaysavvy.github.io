Hand Cannon

$19,300–27,500
Damage250
RPM200
Range1500
Accuracy2.0
Magazine size20

The Hand Cannon is an epic hand cannon. Its forgiving magazine capacity and damage per shot provide heisters a solid suppression option for combat-heavy missions. It is one of Jupiter’s signature weapons.

Appearances

  • Jupiter obtains a Hand Cannon after she completes Jupiter's Gym. She also carries a Hand Cannon when she joins Baker’s crew in multiplayer.
  • Elite hitmen may join Baker’s crew with Hand Cannons.
  • In Baker’s Battle, elite thieves and gangsters may also bring Hand Cannons when Baker hires them.
  • Jupiter may carry a Hand Cannon in King's Treasure and Juiced Up, Iced Out.
  • The Graves may find Hand Cannons when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Hand Cannons to players of any respect level. In multiplayer, Respected or higher players can buy Hand Cannons from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Hand Cannon by completing a medium or long shuffle in multiplayer.
