The DE 44 Elite is a unique legendary hand cannon, a modified DE 44 with increased damage and range. It has extremely high damage output and is a signature weapon of Mr. X, Tuna, and Dollar Dragon.

Appearances

  • Mr. X and Tuna carry DE 44 Elites when Baker hires them.
  • Dragon has a DE 44 Elite when players unlock him in multiplayer.
  • Mr. X may carry a DE 44 Elite in Hauling Ash and The Big Khan, Tuna may carry one in New Blood, and Silver may carry one in Who Took the Dogs Out?.
