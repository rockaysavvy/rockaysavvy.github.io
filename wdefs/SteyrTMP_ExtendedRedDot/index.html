The Sting ER is an uncommon modified Sting which adds a red dot sight to the Sting E’s configuration. The result is a weak SMG … with a red dot sight.

Appearances

  • Veteran thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with a Sting ER.
  • In multiplayer, elite thieves and gangsters may also have Sting ERs when Baker hires them.
  • The Graves may find Sting ERs when they re-take a turf they have previously captured.
  • Low respect, Connected, and Respected players can purchase Sting ERs from the black market, as can campaign players who are Formidable.
  • Players can also obtain a Sting ER by completing a short shuffle in multiplayer.
