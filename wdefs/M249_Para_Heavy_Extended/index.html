The SAW Para is a rare LMG from the Heavy Hitters Pack. Its long range and high damage make it a cost-effective choice for combat-heavy missions.

Appearances

  • In Baker’s Battle, elite hitmen may bring SAW Paras when they join Baker’s crew. In multiplayer, elite thieves and gangsters may have them.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with an SAW Para if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find SAW Paras when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell SAW Paras to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Players who are at least Connected can purchase SAW Paras from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain an SAW Para by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.

Trivia

  • The SAW Para is based on the M249 Para.
  • “SAW” stands for Squad Automatic Weapon.

Known issues

  • The folded bipod in the SAW Para’s icon intersects its foregrip.
