Practical Elite is a legendary shotgun. It comprises a modified Practical with a 3x scope and a choke, which offer substantially improved performance similar to the Urban Striker. With its impressive accuracy and handling, it is a strong shotgun.

Appearances

  • Tuna uses this shotgun in New Blood.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with this shotgun if he has level five of Super Strapped.
