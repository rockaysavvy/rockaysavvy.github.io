The Nail bat is an unlockable uncommon melee weapon from the Heavy Hitters Pack and Stinger’s signature primary weapon, similar to the Crowbar. Its above-average range doesn’t compensate for its inferior precision, making it a poor choice of melee weapon.

Appearances

  • Players who own the Heavy Hitters Pack automatically unlock the Nail bat in multiplayer and receive a Nail bat at the beginning of every campaign playthrough.
  • Stinger has a Nail bat.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Nail bat if he has level two of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Nail bats when they capture a turf or re-take a turf they have previously captured.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect, connected, respected, and formidable players can purchase Nail bats from the black market in the campaign.

Trivia

  • Unlike the Baseball bat, which bears the logo of the Bay Crabs, the Nail bat has a generic “Baseball” logo.
