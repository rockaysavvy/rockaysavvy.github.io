Scorp

$19,000–20,900
Damage120
RPM850
Range1600
Accuracy4.5
Magazine size20

The Scorp is an unlockable rare submachine gun. It behaves like the SMG 9mm with better damage per shot but significantly worse magazine capacity and accuracy, making it a subpar choice of submachine gun.

Appearances

  • Tier I–II Graves and Riders can have Scorps.
  • In turf attacks and defenses, some tier I–II Riders use Scorps.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Scorp if he has level three of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Scorps to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Players who are at least connected can also purchase Scorps from the black market.

Trivia

  • The Scorp is based on the Škorpion vz. 61.
