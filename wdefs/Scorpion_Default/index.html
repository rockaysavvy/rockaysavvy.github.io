Scorp

Damage120
RPM850
Range1600
Accuracy4.3
Magazine size20

The Scorp is a rare SMG. Its high damage per shot somewhat compensates for its small magazine.

Appearances

  • Tier I–II Riders and backup can carry Scorps.
  • Elite thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with Scorps.
  • In Baker’s Battle, veteran thieves and gangsters as well as elite hitmen may also bring Scorps when Baker hires them.
  • Ropes can carry a Scorp in New Blood.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Scorp if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Scorps when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Scorps to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Connected or higher players can purchase Scorps from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Scorp by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.

Trivia

  • The Scorp is based on the Škorpion vz. 61.
