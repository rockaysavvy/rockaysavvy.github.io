The SAM-AF1 is an uncommon assault rifle from the Heavy Hitters Pack with acceptable performance across the board. Its greatest strengths are perhaps its affordability and its availability to players with low respect.

Appearances

  • Players who own the Heavy Hitters Pack receive an SAM-AF1 at the beginning of each campaign playthrough.
  • Tier III Scudos may have SAM-AF1s.
  • Veteran thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with an SAM-AF1.
  • In multiplayer, elite thieves may also have SAM-AF1s when Baker hires them.
  • The Graves may find SAM-AF1s when they re-take a turf they have previously captured.
  • Low respect, Connected, and Respected players can purchase SAM-AF1s from the black market, as can campaign players who are Formidable.
  • Players can also obtain an SAM-AF1 by completing a short shuffle in multiplayer.

Trivia

  • The SAM-AF1 is based on the FAMAS F1.
  • “SAM-AF” is “FAMAS” backwards.
