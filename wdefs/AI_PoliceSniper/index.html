The Police Sniper Rifle is a reserved sniper rifle used by most enemy snipers. Players cannot purchase it or pick it up during missions.

Appearances

  • All tiers of police snipers, as well as all tiers of Sicario, Chico, Rider, and Scudo snipers, use Police Snipers.
  • Snipers in most story missions have Police Snipers. The snipers in Pinned Down and On Rails use a variation of the Police Sniper with no laser.
