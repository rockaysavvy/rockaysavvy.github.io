The Knife is an unlockable common sharp melee weapon and Vamp’s signature primary weapon. It performs like the Crowbar with notably better precision but worse range, rendering it a reasonable choice of melee weapon.

Appearances

  • Santos and Vamp carry Knives.
  • Tier I Graves, Riders, Scudos, and Sicarios may use Knives.
  • In turf wars, tier I Riders and Sicarios carry Knives, and some tier I Scudos also use them.
  • In story missions, Chicos, Riders, Scudos, and prisoners may use Knives.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Knife if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, low respect and connected players can purchase Knives from the black market in the campaign.
  • Generic heisters who offer to join Baker’s crew in the campaign sometimes have Knives.
