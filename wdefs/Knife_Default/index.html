The Knife is a common sharp melee weapon. Its very high attack speed balances its relatively low damage per hit, making it one of the better low-quality melee weapons.

Appearances

  • Tier I Sicarios, Riders, Scudos, and backup can carry Knives.
  • Scudos, Riders, and Chicos can carry Knives in story missions.
  • Santos and several other prisoners attack with Knives in Baller.
  • Rookie thieves and gangsters may have Knives when Baker hires them.
  • Low respect and Connected players can purchase Knives from the black market.
