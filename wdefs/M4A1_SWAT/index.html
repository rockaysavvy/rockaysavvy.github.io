Damage100

Range2700

Magazine size30

Some enemies carry a modified TIMM 4 called the TIMM 4 EHMB. This is not the same weapon as the legendary TIMM 4 EHMB that players can purchase from the black market; it also does not have the modifications that name would normally indicate. Players can only obtain it by picking it up during missions.

Appearances

  • Tier II SWAT officers can carry TIMM 4 EHMBs, as well as the SWAT officers in Operation Red Star.
  • Elite guards may also have TIMM 4 EHMBs.
