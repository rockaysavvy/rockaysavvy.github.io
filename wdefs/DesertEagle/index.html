DE 44

Damage300
RPM240
Range1000
Accuracy2.0
Magazine size10

The DE 44 is an epic hand cannon. Its high damage per shot and forgiving magazine capacity offer heisters a powerful sidearm choice.

Appearances

  • Elite hitmen may join Baker’s crew with DE 44s.
  • In Baker’s Battle, elite thieves and gangsters may also bring DE 44s when Baker hires them.
  • Ropes may carry a DE 44 in King's Treasure and Juiced Up, Iced Out, and Lector may carry one in Who Took the Dogs Out?.
  • Some Skeety Kitty Arms locations sell DE 44s.
  • The Graves may find DE 44s when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell DE 44s to players of any respect level. In multiplayer, Respected or higher players can buy DE 44s from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a DE 44 by completing a medium or long shuffle in multiplayer.

Trivia

  • The DE 44 is based on the Desert Eagle Mark I.
