DE 44

$19,300–27,500
Damage330
RPM261
Range1300
Accuracy2.0
Magazine size10

The DE 44 is an unlockable epic hand cannon and a signature weapon of Dollar Dragon and Lector, similar to the Bearslayer Short. With its superb long-range damage output and reload speed, it’s the best unlockable hand cannon.

Appearances

  • Ropes has a DE 44 in King’s Treasure, Silver uses one in Who Took the Dogs Out?, Tuna wields one in New Blood, and Mr. X uses one in Hauling Ash and The Big Khan.
  • Lector carries a DE 44.
  • Dragon uses a DE 44 in the campaign.
  • Some Skeety Kitty Arms locations sell DE 44s.
  • Players can find DE 44s in the weapons vaults in compound basements.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell DE 44s to players of any respect level in Baker’s Battle.

Trivia

  • The DE 44 is based on the Desert Eagle Mark I.
