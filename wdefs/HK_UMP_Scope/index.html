The BU-RP R is an unlockable legendary submachine gun. It consists of a heavily modified BU-RP with a 3x scope and an extended quickdraw magazine, which offer marginally boosted performance comparable to the PDW O. Even with its awful reload speed, its superb accuracy and overall damage output make it one of the best submachine guns.

Appearances

  • Baker may begin his campaign with a BU-RP R if he has level five of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell BU-RP Rs to respected, formidable, godfather, and crime boss players in Baker’s Battle.
