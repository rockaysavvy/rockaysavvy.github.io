The Vintovka U EH is an unlockable epic assault rifle. It comprises a heavily modified Vintovka U with a holo sight, an extended quickdraw magazine, and a compensator, which offer substantially improved performance over the Vintovka U. It resembles the TIMM 4 EOSG with improved overall damage output and reload speed but worse accuracy and controllability, rendering it a decent choice among the unlockable assault rifles.

Appearances

  • Ropes uses a Vintovka U EH in King’s Treasure.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Vintovka U EH if he has level four of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Vintovka U EHs to players of any respect level in Baker’s Battle. Respected, formidable, godfather, and crime boss players can also purchase Vintovka U EHs from the black market.
