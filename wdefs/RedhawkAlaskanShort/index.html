The Bearslayer Short is a rare hand cannon, a variant of the Bearslayer with a shorter barrel. It is one of Volk’s signature weapons.

Appearances

  • Volk carries a Bearslayer Short when Baker hires him.
  • Detective Gonzales carries a Bearslayer Short.
  • Elite thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with Bearslayer Shorts.
  • In Baker’s Battle, veteran thieves and gangsters as well as elite hitmen may also bring Bearslayer Shorts when Baker hires them.
  • Cracker may carry a Bearslayer Short in King's Treasure, Clutch may carry one in Who Took the Dogs Out?, Peak may carry one in Hauling Ash, Silver may carry one in The Big Khan, and Volk may carry one in Juiced Up, Iced Out.
  • The Graves may find Bearslayer Shorts when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Bearslayer Shorts to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Players who are at least Connected can purchase Bearslayer Shorts from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Bearslayer Short by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.
