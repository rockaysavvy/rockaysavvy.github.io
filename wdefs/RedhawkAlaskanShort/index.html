Bearslayer Short

$11,000–12,100
Damage370
RPM240
Range1500
Accuracy1.6
Magazine size6

The Bearslayer Short is an unlockable rare hand cannon and Volk’s signature secondary weapon. It behaves like the YA-R3K with better handling and reload speed but worse overall damage output and magazine capacity, rendering it a below-average choice among the unlockable secondary weapons.

Appearances

  • Peak uses a Bearslayer Short in Hauling Ash, and Silver carries one in The Big Khan.
  • Detective Gonzales and Volk use Bearslayer Shorts.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell Bearslayer Shorts to low respect, connected, and respected players in Baker’s Battle. Connected or higher players can also purchase Bearslayer Shorts from the black market.
