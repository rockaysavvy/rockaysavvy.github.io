The TIMM 4 RS is a modified TIMM 4 equipped with a suppressor. Its accuracy and controllability make it an excellent choice for conducting stealth assassinations from afar.

Appearances

  • Elite hitmen may join Baker’s crew with TIMM 4 RSs.
  • In Baker’s Battle, elite thieves and gangsters may also bring TIMM 4 RSs when Baker hires them.
  • Some Skeety Kitty Arms locations sell TIMM 4 RSs.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a TIMM 4 RS if he has level two of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find TIMM 4 RSs when they eliminate a gang’s final turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell TIMM 4 RSs to players of any respect level. In multiplayer, players who are at least Respected can buy TIMM 4 RSs from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a TIMM 4 RS by completing a medium or long shuffle in multiplayer.
