The Chopper H is a rare assault rifle, a modified Chopper equipped with a holographic sight and blue camouflage finish. The optic is significantly easier to aim with than the Chopper’s bulky iron sights.

Appearances

  • Elite thieves and gangsters may join Baker’s crew with Chopper Hs.
  • In Baker’s Battle, veteran thieves and gangsters as well as elite hitmen may also bring Chopper Hs when Baker hires them.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with a Chopper H if he has level one of Super Strapped.
  • The Graves may find Chopper Hs when they capture a turf.
  • The Arms Dealer may sell Chopper Hs to Low respect, Connected, and Respected players. Players who are at least Connected can purchase Chopper Hs from the black market.
  • Players can also obtain a Chopper H by completing a short or medium shuffle in multiplayer.

Trivia

  • The Chopper H’s internal name is M16_Extended100_Holo_Default, implying it was originally a Chopper E with a holographic sight.
