SPX

RPM110
Range1100
Accuracy5.5
Magazine size7

The SPX is an unlockable epic pump-action shotgun, in the same class as the Practical. Notwithstanding its exceptional overall damage output, its abysmal controllability, magazine capacity, and accuracy render it one of the least effective epic weapons.

Appearances

  • Jupiter picks up an SPX from the bag her father gives her in Jupiter’s Gym.
  • Jupiter uses the same SPX in Steroid Fire. Following her rampage, she modifies it into her unique weapon.
  • The weapons locker in Cagnali’s office at Cagnali Industries contains an SPX.
  • The weapon locker in Gold Cup contains an SPX.
  • Tier III Graves may use SPXs.
  • Baker may begin his campaign with an SPX if he has level four of Super Strapped.
  • After players unlock the weapon in multiplayer, the Arms Dealer may sell SPXs to players of any respect level in Baker’s Battle. Respected, formidable, godfather, and crime boss players can also purchase SPXs from the black market.

Trivia

  • The SPX is based on the Mossberg 590.
