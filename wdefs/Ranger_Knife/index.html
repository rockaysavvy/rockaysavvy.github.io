The Tactical knife is a unique legendary melee weapon and one of Ranger’s signature weapons. It is one of the strongest melee weapons in the game, along with the Ceremonial dagger. However, it is currently unobtainable.

Appearances

The knife used to be Ranger’s default melee weapon before it was removed.
