Wild Construction is the contracting company Dave works for. He uses the organization both to perform legitimate construction work in Rockay and to provide cover for his criminal undertakings.

Appearances

  • Touchdown hires Dave to cranelift Baker’s crew onto Imperial Bank’s roof when he steals the Dragon Dogs during his campaign. The crane is on site because Wild took over construction on an adjacent development.
  • Wild undertakes a maintenance contract for Central Hospital & Clinics so Dave can plant a shipping container and park the decoy truck for Gloves’s train robbery.
