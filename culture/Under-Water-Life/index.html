Under Water Life

This article is unfinished. It may contain incomplete or inaccurate information.

Under Water Life is a documentary film directed by Theo Van Der Perseen. The film uses extensive deep-sea photography to explore themes of darkness and the unknown. It was released in the summer of 1996.

Appearances

  • The film appears in billboards throughout Rockay City.

  • The studio runs advertisements on K-RCT Rockay City Talk Radio throughout the spring of 1996.

    In a world covered in water there is only one way to go… and that’s under. An under water world will take you to the dark depths of an ocean you’ve never seen, and you will have no idea what lurks behind each crag, crevice, and strand of seaweed. Only in cinemas this summer.
