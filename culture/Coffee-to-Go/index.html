Coffee to Go

“Get us some coffee, we’re gonna need a shitload.”

This article is unfinished. It may contain incomplete or inaccurate information.

Coffee to Go (stylized as Coffee to go) is a popular beverage retailer and chain café in Rockay City. It operates a stall at Rockay Central as well as vending machines in establishments throughout the city.

Strategy

Menu

Self-service machines offer the following beverages:

  • Black coffee
  • “Coffee light”
  • “Cafe late” (a pun on Caffè latte)
  • Cappuccino
  • Caffè mocha
  • French vanilla
  • Hot chocolate (with or without milk)

Kiosks don’t feature a menu but offer a similar selection.
