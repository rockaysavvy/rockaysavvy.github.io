The explosion at King’s skyscraper is a terrorist attack perpetrated by an unknown entity. The explosion originates from the King’s throne in the penthouse and kills King, Mayor Andersson, most of King’s lieutenants, several RCPD higher-ups, and a large number of civilians. In the ensuing chaos, the gang bosses each secure several turfs for themselves and establish feuding criminal enterprises.

Survivors

Casey and Dollar Dragon avoid the attack because they leave the party just before the detonation; likewise, Baker lives because he arrives at King’s skyscraper just as the explosion obliterates the penthouse above him. Tuna, one of the building’s doormen, survives unscathed, perhaps due to his sheer tenacity.
