Summer Drops

“When summer drops, drink Summer Drops!”

This article is unfinished. It may contain incomplete or inaccurate information.

Summer Drops is a popular and heavily-marketed fruit-flavored soft drink brand. The company’s distinctively lurid yellow billboards punctuate Rockay City’s neighborhoods.

Products

The company offers its flagship sweetened fruit-flavored beverage as well as a line of 100% fruit juices with no added sugars:

  • Summer Drops tomato
  • Summer Drops orange
  • Summer Drops watermelon
  • Summer Drops Multivitamin, a blend of several juices

Appearances

  • Eddie’s Groceries sells a number of Summer Drops beverages.
  • Summer Drops sponsored issue 5 of RockON Magazine as well as episodes one, two, and three of K-RCT Rockay City Talk Radio.
