Central Hospital & Clinics is a medical complex in Rockay City, located next to Rockay Central Station.

Appearances

  • Advertisements for Central Hospital & Clinics appear on payphones throughout Rockay.
  • Gloves’s escape plan for the Money Train robbery hinges on Central Hospital & Clinics’s lax security measures. He hires Dave to park a decoy truck and shipping container next to the Diagnostic Center overnight, then pays the Gemini to airlift the container out—along with the loot and Baker’s entire crew.
