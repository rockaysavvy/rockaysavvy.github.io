K-RCT Rockay City Talk Radio

K-RCT Rockay City Talk Radio is a radio show in Rockay City broadcast by Rockay City News Network. It features a variety of programming, including crime and traffic updates, interviews, pop culture segments, local news, and music.

Episodes

  • K-RCT Episode 1: Damion Poitier on Nasara, storytelling, and voice acting; traffic.
  • K-RCT Episode 2: Baker and his crew; CB trailer music.
  • K-RCT Episode 3: console release; flying manatee; travel in Rockay.
  • K-RCT Episode 4: Damion Poitier on writing, acting, and bedtime stories.

Sponsors

Episodes of K-RCT have been sponsored by RockON Magazine, Under Water Life, Skeety Kitty Arms, Grunt’s, Summer Drops, Chicken Barbecue, and the Laffa Motel.
