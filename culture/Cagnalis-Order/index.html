Cagnali’s Order (movement)

This article is unfinished. It may contain incomplete or inaccurate information.

Cagnali’s Order is a political movement and system of martial law instituted by Dr. Cagnali and enforced by her army of robotic police.

Origins

Cagnali’s Order was first announced in an HTML comment on the Cagnali Industries website, encoded using a Caesar cipher with a rightward shift of one.

Freedom is overrated—not suitable for humans. Humanity needs order. Cagnali Industries reveals its true faces on May twenty-first, nineteen ninety-four, during a world-wide broadcast. This will be the beginning of the new order: Cagnali’s Order.
