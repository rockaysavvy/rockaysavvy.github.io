Juiced Up, Iced Out is the second Urban Legend; players must first complete New Blood to unlock it. The Legend follows Baker’s beefiest heisters as they put Hielo in his place for interfering with their exercise routine.

Consequences

Completing the sequence with any number of stars unlocks the achievement Roid Ranger as well as the next Urban Legend, Secret Symphony. Finishing with two or three stars contributes to unlocking Rockay Raider or Rockay Knight, respectively.
