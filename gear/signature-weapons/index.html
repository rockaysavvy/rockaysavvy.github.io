Signature weapons

Each unique character has a fixed set of signature weapons and equipment in multiplayer. These characters usually bring this gear when they join Baker’s crew in Baker’s Battle, although some characters like Ranger and Jupiter don’t obtain their signature weapons until they make progress in their stories during the campaign.
