Weapon modifications

This article is unfinished. It may contain incomplete or inaccurate information.

Weapon modifications affect the appearance and specifications of the various weapons. Players can’t reconfigure the modifications of a weapon in their inventory, but they can purchase or acquire weapons with different modifications.

Nomenclature

Weapon names usually include a series of letters indicating the attached modifications.

NameMeaningExample
EExtended magazineSting E
FFlash hider*Micro SMG EORF
GForegripSLR G
HHolographic sightSAM-AF1 H
RRed dot sightGlizzy R
MBCompensator**Vintovka U
SSuppressorBrescia 92 S

* “F” appears only in the name of the Micro SMG EORF, which used to be incorrectly named the “Micro SMG ES,” and not in other weapons with flash hiders like the Micro SMG E; it was added at my request to distinguish it from the Micro SMG ER.

** “M” and “B” may not be contiguous, as in the Glizzy BEMR.
