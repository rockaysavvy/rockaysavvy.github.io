Black market

“Any two-bit thief with a gun can slang drugs and hit banks.”

The black market sells weapons in both Baker’s Battle and multiplayer. Baker’s respect determines the quantity and quality of weapons he may purchase. Players can access the black market by clicking its icon in the Team menu.

Campaign

Unlike the Arms Dealer, the black market is accessible at all times; however, it sells lower-quality weapons. The randomly selected set of available weapons changes every day, and players can refresh it sooner by paying a fixed cost that depends on Baker’s respect level.

Multiplayer

The list of available weapons is random and changes every thirty minutes. Players can see a new selection sooner for $45,000; this price increases by 20% each time and resets after thirty minutes.

The black market also collects weapons from heisters who perished during previous missions, which players can repurchase at a 50% premium. Unique weapons remain in the store indefinitely, whereas others disappear after three days.
