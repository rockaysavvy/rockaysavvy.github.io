black market

“Oh man, oh gosh…. I got enough guns! I don’t need this!”

The black market sells weapons in Baker’s Battle; Baker’s respect determines the quantity and quality of weapons he may purchase. Players can access the black market by clicking its icon in the Team menu.

The randomly selected set of available weapons changes every day, and players can refresh it sooner by paying a price that depends on Baker’s respect level; this price increases by 75% each time and resets at the end of the day.

Arms dealer

The arms dealer sells higher-quality weapons in the black market and is the only way players can purchase epic and legendary weapons in the campaign. However, he has less inventory and requires Baker to have money available before he agrees to a meeting.

The arms dealer only appears if Baker has enough cash at the beginning of the day, and he never makes an offer on the first day of the campaign. His market remains open for two days or until Baker purchases his entire inventory; then he closes up shop and takes three days to restock with a new, randomized selection. As Baker gains respect, the quantity and quality of available weapons increase:
