The Nemesis

The Nemesis is a shadowy international operative and Cracker’s sworn enemy.

Appearances

After Cracker acquires the briefcase for Nasara, the Nemesis kidnaps and tortures him, departing with the asset and leaving Cracker for dead.

Cracker and Nasara collaborate with the Scientist to locate the Nemesis. They find him in Tora Bora, pretending to sell the briefcase to the terrorist Lafoushini to help the CIA gain access to him. Cracker infiltrates the cave stronghold, executes the Nemesis, recovers the briefcase, and can choose to eliminate Lafoushini too for a hefty CIA reward.
