Dave

Dave is Touchdown’s contact for construction and maintenance work. He leads the window cleaning crew that sets up the zipline in the Hidden Vault heist and provides the improved buzzsaw for Money Train; he also plants the rappel gear and operates the crane in Dragon Dogs.

Trivia

  • Touchdown often calls him “Davey” or “Davie,” implying they know each other well.
