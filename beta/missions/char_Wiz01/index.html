Check Mate

Crew Wiz

Stealth Required

Check Mate is the first mission in What’s on the Disk, Wiz? and a non-canonical mission in Wiz’s story. It’s only available if Baker steals the disc from the meeting. He smuggles a readout to Wiz, who needs to smuggle a response out.

Strategy

Hit the toilet with a chess piece */3

Players can practice tossing pieces outside the cell, but if a guard finds one, he will call in a strike.

Drop a chess piece into the laundry cart */3

There are five chess pieces with hidden messages, so Wiz can afford to miss twice. It’s possible for Wiz to get all three in the cart in one pass, but there’s no rush; the cart will keep coming back until he gets three in or runs out of pieces.

Consequences

The chess pieces contain a note to Baker, asking him to plant a sniffer in a local bank. Once Wiz has the information from the device, he can decode the disk in prison.

If the player ignores or fails the mission, Wiz goes to solitary confinement, and Nasara finds another way to decode the disk in 6–7 days.

Known Issues

  • If players get three strikes, the guards will shoot Wiz through the walls.
