Airlift

Airlift is a story trade mission in Train Robbery. Gloves’s escape plan requires a helicopter to lift Baker’s crew and loot from behind the decoy truck and carry them away from the station. Baker hires the Gemini for $130,000.

Consequences

After Baker completes this mission and Park the Van, Gloves informs him of the next step in his plan: plant a bomb at the train station.
