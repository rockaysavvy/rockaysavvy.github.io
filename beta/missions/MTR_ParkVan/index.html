Park the Van

Park the Van is a preparatory cutscene mission in Train Robbery. Gloves’s escape plan requires a decoy vehicle, so Baker sends an associate to park a truck at the hospital near the train station.

Consequences

After Baker completes this mission as well as Airlift, Gloves informs him of the next step in his plan: plant a bomb at the train station.

Known issues

  • The mission details refer to the truck as a “van.”
