Choppers

Choppers is a story trade mission, the final preparatory mission in Dragon Dogs. Baker needs to hire two helicopters to airlift the Dragon Dogs out of Imperial Bank.

The pilots requested a favor instead of cash as payment: if Baker successfully completed Chase’s story (by providing transportation for Chase and assisting him in obtaining documents), they’ll do the job for free. Otherwise, they require $130,000 up front.

Transcript

Pilot: Sir, our Geminis will whisk you in and out. Clean.

Pilot: You know our price... for the blades and the fuel.

Do you want to pay the pilots to help you during the heist?

    Baker: I know it. Hoist my stuff gentle-like, and we’ll be sweet.

  • Use favor

    Baker: I know it. Hoist my stuff gentle-like, and we’ll be sweet.

  • Not now

Consequences

After Baker hires the choppers, Gloves needs a day to set everything up. Then, his crew can proceed with the heist.
