Plant Ziplines

Plant Ziplines is a preparatory story trade mission in Dragon Dogs. Gloves’s entry plan requires rappelling equipment so Baker’s crew can reach the lower floors of Imperial Bank from the roof. Touchdown’s contact Dave demands payment of $39,000 up front.
