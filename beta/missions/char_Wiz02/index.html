Stealth Required

Soap Up is the third mission in What’s on the Disk, Wiz? and a non-canonical mission in Wiz’s story. It’s only available if Baker successfully plants the snooping device. Wiz decrypts the disk using data from the sniffer and has to smuggle its contents out of prison.

Strategy

If a guard’s detection meter fills at any point, the mission instantly ends in failure.

Pick up the soap

Wiz will occasionally drop the soap; press to pick it up again.

Hide the soap

Players must keep checking vents until one opens; when it does, they should press to stash the soap inside, then close the vent.

Consequences

The contents of the disk allow Gloves to locate the Hidden Vault and plan the heist. If the player ignores or fails the mission, Nasara finds another way to decode the disk in 3–4 days.
