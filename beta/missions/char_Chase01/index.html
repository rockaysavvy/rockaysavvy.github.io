Trespassing

Crew Chase

Stealth Available

Trespassing is the first mission in Chase’s story and a supporting mission in Dragon Dogs. In exchange for piloting the helicopters, the Gemini want Baker to escort Chase to safety after he arrives in America.

Strategy

Chase can maintain stealth if he avoid the cops or dispatches them using his Machete or Chase’s Rifle. Player gunfire doesn’t trigger the alarm; it merely alerts nearby police.
