X Marks the Spot

Main objective Uncover information about the Dragon Dogs

Crew Silver

Stealth Available

X Marks the Spot is the final mission in A Rich Guest, part of the Dragon Dogs plot line. After Baker hires Silver to investigate the Dragon Dogs, she sneaks into a freight yard where the Dogs are temporarily being held to discover their final destination.

Strategy

3–4 security guards patrol the yard, and one may patrol inside the offices. Silver begins the mission on the roof of a nearby building. She should wait before using the zipline until guards are not watching its terminus.

Get into the shipping office unseen

Players can slide along the pipes, gangways, and stacked containers to avoid the guards patrolling below. There are a few civilians in the offices whom Silver can either avoid or intimidate.

Consequences

If Silver completes the mission successfully, she tells Gloves which vault(s) the Dogs will be kept in at Imperial Bank. If she also raises the alarm, the bank’s security measures will be significantly increased.

If the player fails or ignores the mission, Silver will leave the campaign forever, and Nasara will locate the Dogs, after two more days if the mission was ignored. Gloves won’t know which vault(s) the Dogs are in at Imperial Bank, so Baker’s crew will have to drill arbitrarily until they find them.

Known issues

  • If Silver finds and photographs the Dogs before the shipping manifest, Nasara instructs her to escape instead of telling her to enter the offices. After she photographs the manifest, he tells her to find the container even though she already has.
  • Even after Silver raises the alarm, the objective still reads “Get into the shipping office unseen”.
