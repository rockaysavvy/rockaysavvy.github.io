Jewelry Dealer

Jewelry Dealer is a story trade mission in Ringer, part of the Train Robbery plot line. It’s only available if Baker twice fails to steal the engagement ring for Gloves’s police contact, after which Gloves urges him to buy it instead. Nasara’s contact Larry demands $100,000 for the piece, because he has a competing offer from a prince.

Transcript

Do you want to buy the ring for the informant?

Consequences

If Baker purchases the ring, Gloves trades it to his source for the intel he needs and begins planning the heist. If the player ignores the mission, Hielo robs the train instead and obtains $650,000 for himself.
