On Rails

Crew Cracker

Stealth Unavailable

On Rails is the fourth mission in Cracker’s story. After Nasara finds the bomb buried deep in the Arctic, he dispatches Cracker to oversee its excavation.

Strategy

Completing the initial objectives as fast as possible gives Cracker’s backup a better chance of surviving the waves of Spetsnaz. Cracker’s Chopper only has a 20-round magazine, so players may want to switch to semiautomatic to minimize reloads or use his secondary Brescia 92, which has higher short-to-medium–range DPS and reloads faster.

Stay on guard

Cracker begins the mission on a cliff edge; the fall is lethal. Several Spetsnaz appear below, one of whom launches an RPG at Cracker and his compatriot.

Defend the bomb

Cracker can replenish his ammunition using the crates mounted on either side of the sled. Players should quickly kill the Spetsnaz who appear on the ridge and behind the sled, since their numbers can overwhelm Cracker and his backup.

Occasionally the sled's engine may flood; Cracker must hold out while it rests, then restart it.

Hold off the enemies

The ice mounds on the floe offer good cover for Cracker. After he kills the remaining 15–20 Spetsnaz, he can escape by submarine.

Consequences

Completing this mission satisfies the Scientist, who reveals the Nemesis’s location to Nasara; he sends Cracker to exact his vengeance and recover the briefcase. If the player ignores or fails this mission, Cracker dies and his story ends.
