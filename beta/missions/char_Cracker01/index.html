Hook Up

Crew Cracker

Stealth Unavailable

Hook Up is the first mission in Cracker’s story. After he obtains the briefcase, the Nemesis’s men capture Cracker and torture him for the code to unlock it. the Nemesis gives up and leaves with the briefcase, but Cracker can still escape with his life.

Strategy

Grab the gun

There is a Brescia 92 on the workbench opposite the acid bath. Cracker can sustain six strikes from the henchman’s Baseball bat before he loses consciousness and dies.

Shoot the rope

Players can eliminate the henchmen before shooting the rope, but it’s significantly harder to aim while Cracker is upside-down. Moreover, any injuries Cracker sustains before he breaks the rope persist for the duration of the mission.

Eliminate all of them

There are four henchmen in the room: the one armed with the bat and three more each with a Brescia 92.

Get to the car

Consequences

Completing this mission allows Cracker to team up with Nasara to hunt the Nemesis and recover the briefcase by trading favors with the Scientist. If the player ignores the mission or Cracker dies, his story ends and he leaves Baker’s Battle forever.

Trivia

  • Jumping in the acid bath will instantly kill Cracker and fail the mission.
