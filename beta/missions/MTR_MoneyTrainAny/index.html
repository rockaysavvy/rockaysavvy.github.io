Money Train is the final and principal mission in Train Robbery. Gloves’s bomb derails the train, and Baker’s crew descends upon it, but Norris’s men aren’t far behind.

Random events

If Baker’s crew failed to plant the bomb at the station and paid Cracker to do so instead, Norris has a chance of ambushing Baker’s crew at the beginning of the mission. If Baker failed at least one attempt to steal the ring for Gloves’s police informant, Hielo may rob the train if he hasn’t already departed Rockay City.

Strategy

The train keys either drop from one of the security guards around the station upon his death or appear in the control room on the upper level:

Players should either kill the guards very quickly after the mission starts or refrain from bringing AI teammates due to a bug that prevents the key from spawning. If SWAT officers ambush Baker’s crew, the train keys will always be in the control room.

If the bomb overcranked:

Extinguish the Fire

Heisters can find Fire Extinguishers throughout the train station:

Activating the sprinkler system by interacting with the computer in the control room or waiting for it to automatically engage will extinguish the fires outside of the train, but heisters will still need portable extinguishers to douse any flames inside the car.

Break the train doors open

Get to the money

There are two inner cages in the train car; heisters can only saw one at a time. Obtaining the train keys is much faster and lets the crew instantly unlock both doors.

Bag the money

Players can overstack to minimize the number of bags. The hospital is quite far away and heisters run faster than law enforcement, so it’s better to make multiple trips than try to juggle more than two bags per heister across the roadblocks.

If the crew wants to obtain all loot, there are also two cash registers in the station, both near the Woodward Ave.–Handerson St. exit:

Get out of the station

Fight your way to the container

A heavy appears to the left of the hospital, but he walks quite slowly; players can ignore him if he stays afar. The safest route is along the right side of the roadblock; heisters can use the benches, parked cruisers, and dumpsters as cover.

Set off the smoke screen

Throw the bags into the container

Time to escape

Players should ensure all crew members are safe and the immediate area is free of enemies before radioing the pilot, due to a bug in the escape sequence.

Consequences

Completing, failing, or ignoring this mission ends the Train Robbery plot line. If Baker fails or ignores the mission and Hielo robs the train, he obtains $650,000 for the Chicos.

Known issues

  • If an AI crew member kills the guard carrying the key, it will not drop.
  • Crew members can die during the escape cutscene, including Baker, which will end the playthrough.
