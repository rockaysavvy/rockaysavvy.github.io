The Hidden Vault is the final mission in Piggy Bank and the principal mission of The Disk. Baker sends his crew to rob the vault hidden in Vertigo Bank, using the access code from the disk to gain entry to the vault room.

Strategy

Enter the bank

The security room always appears on the staircase landing. Players who wish to proceed stealthily should wait until the coast is clear, pick the lock on the door, and disable the cameras.

Find the board with the shift names

Find the night manager's computer

The computer is in one of the three offices on the ground floor. Players can search for the access card simultaneously to save time.

The vault has a randomized layout: either a single room with shelves on both sides or a room with a gate the player can drill open. There is enough loot outside the gate to meet the minimum requirements, but if the player does want all of the loot, there are also 1–2 small stashes of cash outside the vault in any of these locations:

Move the loot into the Elevator

If the heisters don’t bring at least 6 bags to the roof, either on their backs or in the elevator, they cannot complete the mission successfully, since the escape objective will never activate.

Go to the roof

Set up the zipline

The zipline is at the edge of the roof opposite the elevator. If the alarm was raised, there will be several SWAT already on the roof; the various gangways and ducts provide good cover for the journey across.

Consequences

Completing the heist successfully unlocks the achievement Savvy Seeker. Whether the player succeeds or not, the disk plot line ends.
