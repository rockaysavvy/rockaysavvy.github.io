Rob Khan's Warehouse

Crew 1–4 heisters

Stealth Available

Rob Khan’s Warehouse is the final mission in Coke or Caviar. It’s only available if the player fails or escapes from Import Export. Khan recovers the cocaine from Hielo (if he stole it) and stores it in a local warehouse under heavy guard. Gloves locates the stash and Baker’s crew gears up to reclaim the candy.

Strategy

Infiltrate the warehouse

A multitude of Riders patrol the area: in the containers, around the perimited of the warehouse, on the warehouse floor, in the rooms, and on the mezzanine. There are also several civilians, so players should aim carefully to avoid killing innocents. If the alarm is activated, several more Riders will arrive near the shipping containers in front of the warehouse.

Steal the cocaine

Move the loot to the van

Time to escape

Consequences

Regardless of whether the player succeeds or fails and regardless of whether the alarm was raised, Khan becomes hostile toward Baker for 7 days.
