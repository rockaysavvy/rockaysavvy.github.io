Tight Rope

Crew Wiz

Stealth Unavailable

Tight Rope is the third mission in Free the Wiz, part of the disk plot line, and the second mission in Wiz’s story. After Baker’s crew plants the snooping devices in the bank, Wiz decrypts the disk—but he still needs to get the information to Baker safely.

Walk across the wire

Players should eliminate the current wave of gangsters from the roof before mounting the wire, since they can’t aim or shoot while walking it. When Wiz starts swaying and “Don’t Move” appears, don’t press any movement keys or he will fall to his death.

Consequences

Completing this mission allows Baker to locate the Hidden Vault and begin planning the robbery, as well as allowing Wiz to try to escape the country. If the player ignores or fails this mission, Wiz leaves the campaign forever and the disk storyline ends.

Known Issues

  • Burning the papers or computer before the objective becomes available causes a softlock, since that interaction doesn’t count toward the requirement of 4.
