Construction Meeting

Crew 1–4 heisters

Stealth Available

Construction Meeting is the first mission in The Disk; Baker will randomly encounter either it or Motel Meeting. After Casey discovers the identity of the disk’s seller and the location of the exchange—a nearby construction site—Baker sends his crew to intervene.

Strategy

Interrupt the deal

Whitey begins the mission standing in front of the building. It’s easiest to eliminate him before he goes upstairs.

If the alarm is triggered before the deal goes down, Whitey will flee with the briefcase.

Eliminate Whitey

Get the briefcase

If Whitey hasn’t completed the trade, he will drop the briefcase when he dies. Players can find the cash upstairs in several duffel bags, along with a stash of drugs. If the exchange goes through, Whitey will leave the briefcase upstairs and drop the cash instead upon death.

Time to escape

Consequences

If Baker obtains the disk, Nasara transmits its contents to Wiz in prison, who smuggles his response out. No matter what happens in Wiz’s story, Gloves will eventually decrypt the disk and help Baker plan the Hidden Vault heist.

If the player fails the mission, Baker instead must break Wiz out of prison. This is the only way to complete Wiz’s story and obtain the achievement Geek Out.
