Station Boom

Main objective Plant a bomb unseen

Crew 1 heister

Stealth Required

Station Boom is the final preparation mission for Train Robbery. Baker sends a single heister to infiltrate Rockay Central and plant a bomb to derail the train.

Strategy

Contrary to the description, this mission can be completed successfully even with stealth strikes, hostages, corpses, and broken cameras.

Get to the train platform unseen

Players can avoid the camera near the entrance by heading to the left. A guard patrols the area along with 1–2 civilian workers.

Another camera surveys the room at the top of the ladder, and another 1–2 civilians are working around the platform.

Plant the C4

Time to escape

Consequences

Completing this mission successfully allows Gloves’s train heist to proceed as planned. If Baker’s associate fails to plant the bomb, he instead pays Cracker a hefty sum to plant it, and the chance that Norris ambushes his crew at the train station increases significantly.
