Station Boom

Main objective Plant a bomb unseen

Crew 1 heister

Stealth Required

Station Boom is the final preparation mission for Train Robbery. Baker sends a single heister to infiltrate the train station.

Strategy

Contrary to the description, this mission can be completed successfully even with stealth strikes, hostages, corpses, and broken cameras.
