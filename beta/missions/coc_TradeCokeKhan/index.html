Fake Coke Deal

Baker pretends he's a drug dealer.

Fake Coke Deal is a story trade mission in What’s on the Boat?. After Baker learns about Khan’s yacht, Casey suggests impersonating a rival dealer to gather information and increase Khan’s shipments. Baker needs to have $250,000 to set up the trade, but he doesn’t actually spend it.
