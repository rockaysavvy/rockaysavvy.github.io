Spend Money To Make Money

Crew Wiz

Stealth Unavailable

Spend Money To Make Money is the final mission in Wiz’s story and is only available if he completes Tight Rope successfully.

Strategy

Load the cash */12

Fuel the plane

Hold off the Chicos

Wiz’s rifle (a Scout 14 with a unique skin) has rather severe recoil, so players may find it easier to use his secondary weapon to dispatch the small number of Chicos he encounters. The boxes around the airplane provide good cover, as do the large fuel tanks. Finally, gangster spawns are sparse, so you have plenty of time to eliminate each enemy wave before proceeding with the objectives.

Push the car

Catch the plane

One the engine has started, the front of the plane will kill Wiz instantly. Players should approach from the side if they wish to leave the country.

If Wiz fails to catch the plane, he escapes by boat at the end of the runway.

Get to the boat

Consequences

If Wiz escapes by plane, he leaves the campaign forever but sends a monetary parting gift to Baker. If he misses the plane and escapes by boat, he joins Baker’s crew and never takes a mission cut. Either way, the player unlocks his character in Crime Time as well as the achievement Geek Out.

If the player ignores or fails this mission, Wiz leaves the campaign forever.
