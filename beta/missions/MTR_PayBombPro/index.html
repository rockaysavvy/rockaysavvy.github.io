Bomberman

Bomberman is a story trade mission in Train Robbery. It’s only available if Baker’s associate fails to plant the bomb undetected in Station Boom. Gloves insists that Baker pay a fixer to plant it. Cracker demands $160,000 for this service—even if he’s already on Baker’s crew.

Transcript

Cracker: Sure - I can place your explosive - easy as pie.

Cracker: My fee - in full - up front - and we're in business.

Do you want to let Cracker set up the explosives?

  • Pay

    Baker: Blow it when the train hits the station - not before… You got it? Say it.

    Cracker: After the engine arrives - bombs away…

  • Not now

    Baker: Cool the fuck out - for right now.

    Cracker: Right - I’ve got other shit to handle anyway.

Consequences

If Baker proceeds with the train job after paying to plant the bomb, either SWAT officers or Chicos will ambush his crew at the beginning of the heist.
