Caving

Crew Cracker

Stealth Partially Available

Caving is the fifth and final mission in Cracker’s story.

Strategy

Cracker begins the mission with a Chopper and a unique, otherwise-unobtainable suppressed Glizzy. Players can complete most of the mission stealthily if they avoid or kill the Afghans and livestock; goats and chickens will raise the alarm if Cracker gets too close.

Get close to the house

Enter the sewer pipe

Cracker can enter the house via the sewer or by climbing the sandbags onto the ground floor.

If Cracker raises the alarm:

Get Into the house

Search the house

If Cracker raises the alarm before he reaches the second floor, Lafoushini’s men will attempt to detonate the tunnel entrance. However, the explosion won’t kill Cracker or fully obstruct the entrance.

Enter the cave

Locate the Nemesis

Cracker must proceed through the cave until he finds a ramp leading upward. He can peek through the bars and kill the four Afghans in the room below, then climb down the ladder and open the door into the Nemesis’s stronghold. After he opens the door, the Nemesis will detonate another booby trap and raise the alarm.

Eliminate the Nemesis

Cracker’s Nemesis has substantial health; players can use the doorway for cover, dumping Chopper magazines into him until he falls, then finishing him quickly with Cracker’s secondary Glizzy.

Retrieve the briefcase

The Nemesis drops the briefcase upon his death.

Exilfrate

Cracker can take the door on the left, eliminate Lafoushini for the CIA, and collect the two million dollar bounty on his head or take the door on the right, kill the CIA agents, and escape.

If Cracker pursues Lafoushini:

Go after Lafoushini

A number of Afghans and a Mounted MG separate Cracker from Lafoushini; players should make short work of them in the close quarters of the cave.

Eliminate Lafoushini

Get out

Take the cash

A few CIA agents meet with Cracker and offer him a cash reward. Once he picks up the bag, he can escape by helicopter.

Otherwise:

Get out

Eliminate the agents */5

Undercover CIA agents try to kill Cracker as he heads toward the escape. He must eliminate all of them before he can reach the helicopter.

Time to escape

Consequences

Completing this mission ends Cracker’s story and unlocks the achievement Cracked the Jack. Cracker offers to join Baker’s crew and is automatically promoted to the maximum level. If the player fails or ignores the mission, Cracker dies and his story ends.

Known issues

  • Cracker can jump directly into the house’s second floor, which prevents the Get Into the house and Enter the sewer pipe objectives from ever advancing; however, he can still finish the mission by completing the (invisible) following objectives.
