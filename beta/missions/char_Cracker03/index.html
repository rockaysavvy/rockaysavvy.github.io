Dish Order

Crew Cracker

Stealth Unavailable

Dish Order is the third mission in Cracker’s story. The Scientist sends Cracker to commandeer a radar facility and scan the Wolf B’s flight path for the bomb.

Strategy

Cracker can land near the cliff edge as Nasara recommends or wait a skosh and land directly on the facility’s roof—a safe and faster route into the facility. If he waits too long and lands by the gatehouse, the Contras there will spot him and raise the alarm.

Enter the building

If Cracker lands on the roof, he can proceed down the staircase and into the facility. Otherwise, he should sneak past the Contras and cameras outside and enter the facility from the ground.

Inside are several Contras and civilian workers; Cracker can take out the soldiers with his Brescia 92 S and shout down (or kill) the civilians as well.

Set up the laptop

The laptop hack will raise the alarm after a few seconds if Cracker hasn’t already.

Protect the laptop 03:00.00

Waves of troops will storm the facility and attempt to interrupt the scan. Cracker can either hunker down near the laptop, using desks and pillars as cover, or take the staircase to the roof and defend the laptop through the narrow aperture of the skylights. He can replenish his ammunition using the crates in the area:

Pick up the laptop

Jump off the cliff

Cracker can circumvent most of the enemies by returning to the roof and descending the ladder, then sprinting the short remaining distance to the cliff edge.

Consequences

Completing this mission successfully allows Nasara to locate the bomb, buried in the Arctic circle; he sends Cracker to excavate it for the Scientist. If the player ignores or fails the mission, Cracker dies and his story ends.
