Snooping Devices

Main objective Plant snooping devices

Crew 1 heister

Stealth Required

Snooping Devices is the second mission in Free the Wiz, part of the disk plot line. It is a more challenging version of Snooping Device. After Wiz successfully escapes prison, he asks Baker to plant three listening devices in a local bank.

Strategy

The location is a Rockay Deposit Reserve branch identical to a standard bank heist, but there is no loot—the player should escape as soon as the devices have been planted. If the alarm is activated, heisters can still escape, but the mission cannot be completed successfully.
