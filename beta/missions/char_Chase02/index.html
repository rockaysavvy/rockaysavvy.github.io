The Predator

Crew Chase

Stealth Unavailable

The Predator is the second mission in Chase’s story and a supporting mission in Dragon Dogs. In exchange for piloting the helicopters, the Gemini want Baker to help Chase obtain a passport.

Strategy

Get upstairs

There are five gangsters downstairs and four on the stairs. Chase’s Rifle and YA-R3K are both more than powerful enough to dispatch them.

Enter the room

The wall explodes next to Jake, but he survives.

Get in

There is one more gangster alive in the room.

Get the papers

Take the guns

Get out

A gangster surprises Chase on the staircase, along with something else.

Wait for the escape van 02:00.00

Chase’s YA-R3K does significantly more DPS than his rifle, because the rifle is much slower to reload; however, it has lower ammo pickup. Players should switch between the two to hold back the waves of police officers.

30 seconds before the escape van reaches Chase, SWAT will arrive, including from the alley opposite the apartment entrance. Hiding out in the entrance to The Bad Scout Pizza is safest, but offers few options for retreat.

Time to escape

Consequences

If Chase escapes, the Gemini offer their piloting skills to Baker at no cost; Chase also offers to join Baker’s crew and the player unlocks the achievement Ding Dong. If the player fails or ignores the mission, Chase dies and the Gemini demand cash. Either way, Baker can proceed with the Dragon Dogs heist.
