Window Cleaners

Window Cleaners is a story trade mission in Piggy Bank. It’s the only required preparatory mission before the heist itself. Gloves’s getaway plan requires a zipline that can withstand the weight of Baker’s crew and their loot—and the window cleaners can install it in advance. Touchdown’s contact Dave demands a $39,000 fee for the service.
