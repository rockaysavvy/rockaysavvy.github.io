Import Export is the principal mission of Coke or Caviar. Casey’s girls board the Miss Olga to distract Khan’s men, but things turn bloody when Norris interferes with Baker’s plans to steal its payload.

Strategy

Two detectives will arrive 10 minutes into the heist and actively search for Baker’s crew, so players should not waste time.

Infiltrate the area

The door to the abandoned store next to the van can be shot or bashed open if the fence opening is guarded by police.

Find the pier

Get onto the pier

There are several options for proceeding past the gate:

  • Players can kill the guard, hide his body, and pick up the key he drops, as Casey suggests. However, doing so fails the “Do not kill anyone” bonus objective.
  • If the ladder spawns beside the building on the far side of the pier, heisters can climb it and drop down either into the warehouse through the window (the intended route) or onto the fence (a safer route).
  • Occasionally, the police officer simply leaves the gate open.

Get onto the boat

The police on the pier can significantly slow progression. Players can run along the second, lower pier (if it spawns) or walk on the outside of the railings and use water warping to avoid detection.

Find the cocaine

If a heister alerts a guard aboard the Olga, he should use strike spam to eliminate other guards on the floor and toss their bodies into the harbor.

Consequences

Completing this mission concludes Coke or Caviar. If the alarm is triggered, Baker becomes Khan’s enemy for 7 days. Otherwise, Khan assumes Hielo stole the coke and blames him instead. If the player ignores or fails the mission, Gloves suggests robbing Khan’s warehouse instead.
