Snooping Device

Main objective Plant a snooping device

Crew 1 heister

Stealth Required

Snooping Device is the second mission in What’s on the Disk, Wiz?. It is an easier version of Snooping Devices. After Wiz smuggles his request out of prison, he asks Baker to plant a listening device in a local bank.

Strategy

The location is a Rockay Deposit Reserve branch identical to a standard bank heist, but there is no loot—the player should escape as soon as the device has been planted. If the alarm is activated, heisters can still escape, but the mission cannot be completed successfully.

Consequences
