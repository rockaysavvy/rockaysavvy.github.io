Jet Set

Crew Cracker

Stealth Available

Jet Set is the second mission in Cracker’s story. The Scientist has intel on the briefcase, but first he wants Cracker to infiltrate a Russian military installation and steal the flight recorder from a fighter jet.

Strategy

Cut the fence

Several Russians patrol the runway, hangar, and barracks, and a lookout occupies each of the two sentry towers. If Cracker wishes to proceed stealthily, he should use his secondary Brescia 92 S to dispatch the guards.

If a searchlight illuminates Cracker or a corpse, the lookouts will instantly raise the alarm. Cracker can kill them to disable the lights, but if he shoots either light directly it will break and instantly raise the alarm, regardless of whether the lookouts are alive.

Locate the pilot

The pilot is in one of the buildings across runway; the other building houses several Russian troops. If Cracker hasn’t raised the alarm, he can cut the second fence and slip through. Otherwise the gates will open and more Russians will arrive; there are mounted MGs above both gates which Cracker can use to eliminate the enemies.

Get the Key

Unlock the jet

Consequences

Completing this mission successfully allows the Scientist to examine the jet’s flight path and narrow his search area; he then asks Cracker to sneak into a radar facility to pinpoint the location of the bomb. If the player ignores or fails the mission, Cracker dies and his story ends.
