Rockay Deposit Reserve

Rockay Deposit Reserve is one of the largest commercial banks in Rockay City. It operates branches throughout the city and is a frequent target of Baker’s crew.

Appearances

  • Baker’s crew regularly robs the vaults at RDR branches and the cash registers at RDR locations in shopping centers.
