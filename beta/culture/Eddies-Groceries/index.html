Eddie’s Groceries

Eddie’s Groceries is a convenience store chain in Rockay City. It has locations in strip malls throughout the city, which Baker’s crew regularly ransacks for money.

Strategy

Heisters cannot steal alcohol or other products from the shelves; the only loot in the store is in the cash register behind the counter.
