Imperial Bank

Imperial Bank is a large private bank in Rockay City. Its distinctive golden logo adorns the coastal skyline, and its rooftop lounge, ornate marble-and-glass interior, comprehensive security, and capacious vaults offer wealthy clientele a uniquely luxurious banking experience.

Appearances

  • The Zang family stores its sizable fortune, including the Dragon Dogs, in the bank’s main vaults below ground level; during Baker’s Battle, Baker may try to steal them by airlifting the Dogs out of the domed skylight in the lobby.
  • The bank may contain a hidden vault full of federal government assets. Baker can access the vault by finding and decrypting the contents of the disk.
