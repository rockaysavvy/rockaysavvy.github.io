Coffee to go

Coffee to go is a popular beverage retailer and chain café in Rockay City. It operates a stall at Rockay Central as well as vending machines in establishments throughout the city.

Strategy

Some Coffee to go kiosks have a cash register heisters can bash or shoot open and loot.

Menu

Self-service machines offer the following beverages:

  • Black coffee
  • “Coffee light”
  • Caffè latte
  • Cappuccino
  • Caffè mocha
  • French vanilla
  • Hot chocolate (with or without milk)

Kiosks don’t feature a menu but offer a similar selection.

Trivia

  • The latte button on the vending machines is misspelled “Cafe Late.”
