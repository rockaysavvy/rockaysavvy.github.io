Summer Drops

Summer Drops is a popular and heavily-marketed fruit-flavored soft drink brand. The company’s distinctively lurid yellow billboards are ubiquitous in Rockay City.

Products

The company offers fruit-flavored soft drinks as well as a line of 100% fruit juices with no added sugars:

  • Summer Drops tomato
  • Summer Drops orange
  • Summer Drops watermelon
  • Summer Drops Multivitamin, a blend of several juices

Appearances

  • Eddie’s Groceries sells a number of Summer Drops beverages.
  • Summer Drops sponsored issue 5 of RockON Magazine.
