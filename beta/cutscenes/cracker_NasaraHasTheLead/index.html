Jet Set: Intro

The Scientist offers information in exchange for a favor.

Nasara: Cracker, a word.

Nasara: Tell me of this, 'scientist'…

Cracker: A very connected bloke, with mutual interests.

Nasara: And his temperament?

Cracker: He’ll collaborate with anyone. But marches to his own drum.

Nasara: Understood.

Cracker: His offer - the location of the briefcase and the whereabouts of your 'nemesis'.

Nasara: Acceptable. Let us proceed.
