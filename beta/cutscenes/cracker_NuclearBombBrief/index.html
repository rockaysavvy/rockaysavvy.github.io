On Rails: Briefing

Nasara briefs Cracker on the bomb excavation.

Nasara: Cracker… It has been some time.

Cracker: Yes sir.

Nasara: That was quite the exit.

Cracker: I’ve had worse.

Nasara: I'm sure.

Nasara: Your next assignment, I understand, is a most sought after object.

Cracker: I read it. Expedition. We go in with zodiacs, dig round for the object. One week’s time for excavation and extraction.

Nasara: Indeed. The scientist has confirmed the use of an appropriated soviet submarine for exfil.

Cracker: Fair enough. Hostiles?

Nasara: None confirmed. Still, join the dig and lead the protection detail.

Cracker: Roger that, Captain. Back in a week - or so.
