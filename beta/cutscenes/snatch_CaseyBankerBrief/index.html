Casey Banker Brief

Baker: Banker is giving you a hard time?

Casey: The scumbag that helped us invest the money?

Casey: He threatens to tell the whole thing to the press.

Baker: He is a banker. He wants money, no?

Casey: Yes… We can either chicken out and pay him what he wants – or…

How do you suggest we best deal with the banker?

  • Pay

    Baker: Casey… if there is one thing I know: It is that banks all win in the end… pay him.

    Casey: Wow… ok…. so we just take it then… wow…

  • Kill the banker

    Baker: Wait, a “banker” is extorting us? Yeah, that’s not gonna fly – I’ll close his account.

    Casey: Now you talking.

  • Decline

    Baker: Listen, I don’t care about this Snatcher IPO – let him blow the lid…

    Casey: That’s bad. Image and all…

    Baker: Don't care for the vultures. Gonna hit a bank today for all I know.
