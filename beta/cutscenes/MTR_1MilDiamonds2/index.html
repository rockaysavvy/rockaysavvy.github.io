One Million Diamonds: Trade

Casey unloads some diamonds on Hielo.

Slider: Hola Chiquita. So Gaby tells me the ol' Cowboy wanna slang some rocks, huh?

Casey: Uh-huh… all or nothing - my man doesn’t do anything half-assed.

Slider: Ain’t no half-steppin. Hielo’s fuckin loaded - buys it up for sport.

Do you want to sell your diamonds to Hielo?

  • Sell all

    Casey: Alright - have a look - I’m prepared to unload every single piece…

    Slider: Easy - he’ll take it. Hielo’s all that - and a bag of chips.

    Casey: Deal. Just do me a kindness, Slider - let me keep a few of these - as a memory?

    Slider: Aiight, Mama … maybe you and me - we make us some more...

    Casey: Hmmm … I never say never.

    Slider: Our club - anytime, Case. My-man be cookin-up something big. You might see me back for more - hella more.

    Casey: Always welcome. Love to Gaby.

  • Sell half

    Slider: So you’re just here to see my pretty face? Hielo gon' be mad disappointed… Yo - Gaby already told me you holdin that top-shelf bling. The fuck, Case…?

    Casey: Sorry, Slider - not this time…

    Slider: You wasting your life with that Elvis wannabe mutherfucker… come roll with a real crew, yo.

  • Decline

    Slider: Come on, Case… I know he’s got more…

    Casey: Oh Baker’s got more alright…

    Slider: Case - page me, aiight?

    Casey: I wouldn’t let go of all these beauties - but Baker might.
