A Rich Guest: Deal

Silver offers Baker the information, for a price.

Silver: Yes, hello?

Baker: Yeah, listen. Nasara gave me your digits.

Silver: Oh, you want the skinny on the Zangs, eh? I can give you some real ‘juicy’ stuff.

Silver: Is my price cool?

Do you want Silver to find out information about the Zangs?

  • Agree on payment

    Baker: If it's juicy, yeah. We got a deal.

    Silver: So juicy it’s dripping. I'll swing by in the next couple of days.

  • Decline

    Baker: No dice, little birdie. Not enough Zang for my buck.

    Silver: Suit yourself. You think better of it, I’ll be around for a few days.
