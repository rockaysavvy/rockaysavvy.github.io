The Predator: Intro

Eduardo and Chase set off to obtain counterfeit passports.

Nasara: I need you to deliver the Coke and get Chase the weapons and the passports.

Eduardo: You go with Chase… he is not real in the head, man!

Nasara: We really need passports for the guys in the Graves. Only the Eastmen have them.

Eduardo: There are plenty of guns around…

Nasara: Not guns! The passports, idiot. Go see the Eastmen!

Nasara: You want to work the labs instead?

Eduardo: Sure… I go.

Nasara: Make sure Chase trades – I don't want any shoot-outs. Get the papers!
