Loanshark: Consequences

The loan shark did some damage to Baker's operations.

Casey: Our loan shark friend no longer plays nice and upped the pressure on us. I’ll give you a summary of the damages, but first thing first – should we repay the dept?

Are you ready to reimburse the loanshark?

  • Pay

    Baker: Fine, already! Wire him the fucking money, so he can choke on it for all I care.

    Casey: I think it’s a better decision in the long run. We don’t need more headaches at this stage.

  • Send assassins

    Baker: If he thinks he can blackmail us, he’s got another thing coming.

    Baker: Get me his schedule. I don’t care how. He wants to play nasty, I’ll send my boys to teach him how it’s done.

  • Decline

    Baker: Fair’s fair, but this is getting out of hand.

    Casey: It is a big sum of money that we owe him.

    Baker: Which is the only reason why he’s not already at the bottom of the harbor with some nice new cement shoes.
