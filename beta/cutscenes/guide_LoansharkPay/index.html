Loanshark: Time to Pay

The loan shark wants his money back.

Casey: Hello. Today I received a friendly reminder from our loan shark friend that it’s time to repay us what we owe him. With considerable vigorish.

Are you ready to reimburse the loanshark?

  • Pay

    Baker: Pay him. I hope I won’t need that vulture’s services any more.

    Casey: So do I, Boss.

  • Decline

    Baker: How does he expect us to repay him so soon after the loan? That defeats the entire purpose.

    Baker: Get us more time. The vulture will get his money, but he needs to wait some more.

    Casey: He’ll no doubt have plenty of understanding.
