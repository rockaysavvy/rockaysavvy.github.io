Luxuries Or Antiques

Casey: Nasara, gotta talk to you in private.

Nasara: Of course, Casey.

Casey: We will be sitting on a boatload of cash. If the word gets out, it might become an issue.

Nasara: Yes, I’ve noticed. Baker should start investing into assets.

Casey: Exactly! We should start suggesting more substantial stuff.

Casey: I was thinking real estate, unique cars, boats.. He’s gotta project success.

Nasara: Luxuries? Hmm, feels a bit shortsighted. Those things lose value the moment you buy them.

Nasara: I would suggest going with antiques. Such valuables increase your wealth every year.

Casey: What’s he, an old fart now? He needs to be seen by Rockay as a King.

Casey: Wealth on display attracts contacts, crew, and respect in this town.

Nasara: Glitter only attracts women and trouble.

Casey: Museum artifacts is for senior citizens and bores.

Would you like to pursue antique or luxury assets?

  • Antiques

    Casey: Fine! Don’t blame me when the city starts talking about “a peasant boy wanting to be King”.

    Nasara: You are blowing this out of proportion. We should get on antiques now.

    Nasara: I’m certain you know a guy.

  • Luxuries

    Casey: You go and collect stamps all you want, but leave Baker's image to me.

    Casey: I’ll set him up with some sexy things. He and everyone else, will love it.

    Nasara: I’ll concede this to you. I’ll leave this to your…better judgement.
