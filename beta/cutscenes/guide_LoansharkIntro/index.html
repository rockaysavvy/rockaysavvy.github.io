Loanshark: Intro

Baker weighs taking out a predatory loan.

Casey: Look, the way things are going right now, our little kingdom will fall apart. And I’m sure you’re just as unwilling to let it happen.

Casey: I’ve contacted a guy. A little on the dangerous side, but with enough resources to make our financial troubles disappear for a while.

Baker: And what’s in it for him?

Casey: A hefty sum on top of the original loan, naturally. Besides that, he invested well after the late King’s demise, and has a vested interest in keeping the turf war going.

Casey: Then again, once we take the deal, we should expect him to come hard after his money when the time comes to pay.

Do you want to borrow money from a loanshark?

  • Accept loan

    Baker: We can worry about that in the future. The problems we have are now.

    Baker: Let’s take the money, and make ten times more!

    Casey: Sounds like a plan.

  • Decline

    Baker: I won’t be kissing feet of any loan shark. It’s just a minor setback, and I can get us through.

    Casey: As you wish, Boss. Then again, the graveyard is filled with people who couldn’t part with their pride.
