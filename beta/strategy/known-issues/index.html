Interaction softlock

Interacting the world (e.g., placing C4 or zip-tying a hostage) can phase heisters through walls.

Quit to retry

Quitting the game mid-mission restarts the mission the next time the game mode is resumed instead of failing the mission.

Incorrectly accessible turf war area

The indoor area of a certain turf war map is blocked off by stacked chairs and tables and is intended to be inaccessible; however, it can be accessed by breaking windows and jumping in from the building exterior.

Duplicate unique characters

After the player unlocks level 7 of the Plus One skill, the campaign begins with a unique character in the crew. However, this character also becomes available for hire in the normal manner, so the player can hire two of the same unique character.
