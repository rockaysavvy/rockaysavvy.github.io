Strike spam

Stealth strikes are issued for a particular offense after a grace period. For example, a heister can kill any number of guards in quick succession and receive at most one strike. But if he kills a guard and breaks a camera, he will receive up to two strikes.

Technique

In avoid-the-police missions , heisters can eliminate all police while maintaining stealth. Police don’t respond to shouting
