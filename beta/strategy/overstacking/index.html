Overstacking

The loot system allows heisters to carry 2–3 full bags each; however, they can carry significantly more loot than this limit implies. Each heister can fill two bags, throw one, partially fill a third, then pick up the thrown bag in order to carry up to bags of loot at once.

Mechanics

A heister carrying fewer than the maximum number of bags can fill the loot bar by collecting additional loot; once the bar fills, another bag is added to his inventory, which he can throw anywhere by pressing .

Even with a partially-filled loot bar, each heister can still pick up the maximum number of filled-and-thrown bags. The partial bag of loot is instantly added to the drop point when a heister stands near enough to it; AI-controlled teammates will also automatically run toward the drop point and deposit partial bags.
