Wiz's story

Humberto Wamba plans a vacation.

Wiz's story is a character story in Baker's Battle. It follows Humberto Wamba, an eccentric hacker, as he offers his technical skills to Baker and gets in over his head. The story begins during the disk plot line, after Baker learns of the disk and attempts to acquire it from Whitey. Wiz's main story is only available if Baker fails Construction Meeting or Motel Meeting; otherwise, a shorter, alternative story plays out.

Consequences

Completing the main story is the only way to unlock Wiz in multiplayer and obtain the achievements Geek Out and Full House. If the player fails Baller or Tight Rope (but not Spend Money To Make Money), the disk storyline will also end.

The alternate story is entirely optional, since Nasara will eventually decrypt the disk even if Wiz fails every mission, and Wiz will remain in prison regardless.
