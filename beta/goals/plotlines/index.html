Plot lines

Plot lines are major developments in Baker’s Battle. They generally require significant upfront investment from Baker, but yield larger rewards than moneymaking missions if he completes them successfully.

Mechanics

Plotlines are randomly selected from the available list. The first plotline begins after 0–1 days, the second 2–3 days later, and each subsequent plotline after another 1–2 days. Multiple plot lines can progress simultaneously.
