Plot lines

Plot lines are major developments in Baker’s Battle. They generally require significant upfront investment from Baker, but yield larger rewards than moneymaking missions if he completes them successfully.

Mechanics

Plot lines are randomly selected from the available list. The first plot line begins after 0–1 days, the second 2–3 days later, and each subsequent plot line after another 1–2 days. Multiple plot lines can progress simultaneously.
