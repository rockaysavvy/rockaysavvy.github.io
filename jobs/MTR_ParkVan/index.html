Park a Truck

This article is unfinished. It may contain incomplete or inaccurate information.

Park a Truck is a preparatory cutscene mission in Train Robbery and Armored Cash. Gloves’s escape plan requires a decoy vehicle, so Baker sends an associate to park a truck at the hospital near the train station.

Consequences

After Baker completes this mission as well as Airlift, Gloves informs him of the next step in his plan: planting a bomb at the train station.
