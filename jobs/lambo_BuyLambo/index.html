Papers, Please

Papers, Please is a story trade mission in Luxury Horsepower. After Baker’s crew drops the ball at the DMV, Casey contacts Clutch to make the new car street-legal. He asks $75,000 for the job.

Transcript

Clutch:Hey maje ... Name’s Clutch. I heard from Casey - you need to re-register some hot wheels?

Nasara:Correct. We need someone reliable and discreet.

Clutch:Gonna cost you, maje - but I get you fixed plates and fresh registration.

Clutch:You want it?

Do you want to pay Clutch to re-register your new vehicle?

Consequences

If Baker pays Clutch, he acquires the Torini 3000 for the current campaign playthrough. Clutch also offers to join Baker’s crew and is permanently added to the hiring pool for new campaign playthroughs. This is the only way to unlock Clutch in Baker’s Battle.

If Baker declines the offer, Casey fences the car and he receives a partial refund of $250,000.

Trivia
