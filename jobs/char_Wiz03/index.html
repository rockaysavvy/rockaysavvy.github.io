Baller is the first canonical mission in Wiz’s story. After Nasara’s plan to intercept the disk fails, Baker suggests breaking Wiz out of Rockay Plains Correction Facility so he can help.

Strategy

Play ball */5

The basketball minigame is fun but has no effect on the mission. The explosives will detonate after some time passes or after Wiz shoots five balls.

Escape the prison

After the wall explodes, players should sprint out of the prison to avoid gunfire.

Get to the house

Players should look both ways before crossing the street—cars are quite lethal.

Clear to escape

Time to escape

Consequences

Completing this mission allows Baker to work with Wiz to decrypt the disk. If the player ignores or fails the mission, Wiz dies and the disk storyline ends.
