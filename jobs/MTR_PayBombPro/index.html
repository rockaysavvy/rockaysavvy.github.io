Bomberman

Expires after3 days

This article is unfinished. It may contain incomplete or inaccurate information.

Bomberman is a preparatory story trade mission in Train Robbery and Armored Cash. It’s only available if Baker’s associate fails to plant the bomb undetected in Depot Detonation. Gloves insists that Baker pay a fixer to plant it. Cracker demands $93,600 for this service—even if he’s already on Baker’s crew.

Consequences

If Baker proceeds with the train job after paying to plant the bomb, either SWAT officers or Chicos will ambush his crew at the beginning of the heist.
