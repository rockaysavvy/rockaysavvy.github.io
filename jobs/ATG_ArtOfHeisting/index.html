Hit on Repair Shop is the first mission in the Urban Legend New Blood. Touchdown and Nasara send Baker’s new recruits to pillage Martin’s Station, a local repair shop controlled by Khan’s men. The job proves surprisingly lucrative, since the Riders use the shop for more than the usual kind of automobile work.

Strategy

Infiltrate Riders’ Hideout

Several Riders begin the mission at the front gate, and several more patrol inside and near the entrances. The Riders have hijacked an armored truck and parked it in one of the garage bays.

Loot the van

Take all the Riders' stuff

Unfortunately for Baker’s crew, Khan’s men have already plundered the truck and distributed the money throughout the shop with their other loot:

Players can overstuff loot bags and overstack loot to minimize trips to the escape van, since there is a large quantity of loot.

Locate the plans

The Riders have plans for an upcoming armored truck heist stashed in the safe at Martin’s, either in the waiting area or in the back office. Players should incapacitate all of Khan’s men before starting the drill, lest they hear it and investigate.

Steal the plans from the safe

Move the loot to the escape van

Consequences

The plans from the safe provide Baker’s newest crew members with their next target. Just a few hours after the Martin’s break-in, Nasara and Touchdown send the greenhorns to intercept the cash transport Khan had his eyes on.
