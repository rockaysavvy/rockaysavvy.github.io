Rob Khan’s Warehouse

“Hit that motherfucker—hard and fast.”

Crew1–4 heisters

StealthSupported

Rob Khan’s Warehouse is the final mission in What’s on the Boat?. It’s only available if Baker’s crew fails Import Export or escapes without securing enough loot. Khan recovers the cocaine from Hielo (if he stole it) and stores it in a local warehouse under heavy guard. Gloves locates the stash and Baker’s crew gears up to reclaim the drugs.

Strategy

Infiltrate the warehouse

A multitude of Riders patrol the area: in the containers, around the perimeter of the warehouse, on the warehouse floor, in the rooms, and on the mezzanine. There are also several civilians, so players should aim carefully to avoid killing innocents. If the alarm is activated, several more Riders will arrive near the shipping containers in front of the warehouse.

Steal the cocaine

Move the loot to the van

Time to escape

Consequences

Regardless of whether the player succeeds or fails and regardless of whether the alarm was raised, Khan becomes hostile toward Baker for 7 days.
