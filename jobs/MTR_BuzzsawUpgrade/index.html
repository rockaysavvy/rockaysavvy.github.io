Time Saver

Time Saver is an optional story trade mission in Train Robbery and Armored Cash. Touchdown’s contact Dave offers a significantly faster saw for breaking into the train car. The equipment only costs $18,720, so players should purchase it if they have the cash: the train platform is quite exposed, so the less time heisters spend on it the better their chances of success.
