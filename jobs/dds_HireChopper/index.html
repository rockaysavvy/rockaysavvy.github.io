Choppers

This article is unfinished. It may contain incomplete or inaccurate information.

Choppers is a story trade mission and the final preparatory mission in A Rich Guest. Baker needs to hire two helicopters to airlift the Dragon Dogs out of Imperial Bank.

The pilots requested a favor instead of cash as payment: if Baker successfully completed Chase’s story (by providing transportation for Chase and assisting him in obtaining documents), they’ll do the job for free. Otherwise, they require $78,000 up front.

Consequences

After Baker hires the choppers, Gloves needs a day to set everything up. Then, his crew can proceed with the heist.
