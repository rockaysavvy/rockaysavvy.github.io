Trespassing is the first mission in Chase’s story. He and Manolo arrive in America bearing plenty of contraband, but they need transportation into the city, so Nasara sends several Graves to escort them.

Strategy

Chase can maintain stealth if he avoids the cops or dispatches them using his Fists or his Rifle. Player gunfire doesn’t trigger the alarm; it merely alerts nearby park rangers.

Find the cabin

The cabin lies across the ravine, which Chase can only cross via the log bridge at the far end of the swamp. After about one minute and thirty seconds, Jake will light a fire at the cabin, making it significantly easier to locate for both Chase and the park rangers.

Go to the cabin

If Jake lit the fire but the alarm was not raised, two park rangers will storm the cabin.

Consequences

After Chase reaches the Graves, Jake drives everyone back to the Stallion Club to regroup. Nasara then dispatches Chase, along with Eduardo, Jake, and Martinez, to trade with the East Men and obtain passports and guns.

If the player fails or ignores the mission, Chase dies.
