Jewelry Dealer is a story trade mission in Train Robbery, part of the plot line. It’s only available if Baker twice fails to steal the engagement ring for Gloves’s police contact, after which Gloves urges him to buy it instead. Nasara’s contact Larry the Jeweler demands $62,400 for the piece, because he has a competing offer from a Prince.

Transcript

Do you want to buy the ring for the informant?

  • Buy the ring
  • Pay with luxuries
  • Not now

Consequences

If Baker purchases the ring, Gloves trades it to his source for the intel he needs and begins planning the heist. However, there is a 12% or greater chance Hielo will also attempt the train heist if he hasn’t fled Rockay City.

If the player ignores the mission, Hielo robs the train instead and obtains $650,000 for himself.

Trivia

  • A longer version of the cutscene included a conversation between Baker and Larry. It was removed prior to release.
