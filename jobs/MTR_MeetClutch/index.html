Meet Clutch

This article is unfinished. It may contain incomplete or inaccurate information.

Meet Clutch is a cutscene mission in Clutch’s story. It’s only available the first time Baker completes Papers, Please. The day after Clutch delivers the forged documents for his Torini 3000, he requests another meeting with Baker: he wants to join the crew.

Transcript

Clutch:Hey Bossman, appreciate the time.

Baker:Burnin' money - tick-tock, tick-tock, tick-tock.

Clutch:I'll get straight to the point.

Clutch:I wanna work for you...

Baker:No, I know, I wa-, I wanna get a little more uuh…

Clutch:I am loyal, principled-

Baker:Tell me, Clutch, can you whip a car?

Clutch:Whip, flip, slip away, fast car, stock car, Nascar - Hahaha… The Clutch can do anything with any set of wheels, Bossman!

Do you want to let Clutch be a part of your crew?

  • Accept

    Baker:Alright, you’re on. But you’d better kick it in the ass.

    Clutch:I won't let you down, Boss. Andele!

  • Decline

    Clutch:I get it, Bossman. Love to work for you - anytime.

    Baker:I’ll keep that in mind.

Consequences

Regardless of whether Baker accepts Clutch’s offer, Clutch will have a chance to appear in the hiring pool in future campaign playthroughs starting from day 1; the player doesn’t need to complete Papers, Please more than once.
