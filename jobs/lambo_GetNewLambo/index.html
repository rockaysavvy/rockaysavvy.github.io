Cut the Line is the first mission in Luxury Horsepower. After Casey convinces Baker to buy a Torini 3000, he needs to alter DMV records to make it street-legal.

Strategy

Contrary to the description, this mission can be completed successfully even with hostages, stealth strikes, and broken cameras. However, if a heister kills a guard or civilian, the mission will instantly end in failure.

Get into office B2 unseen

Players begin the mission in a closet, directly behind a guard; they should either intimidate him or wait for him to walk into the kitchen, then proceed through the hallway and down the stairs. There is also a civilian in high-vis in the kitchen.

Alter the vehicle registration

The security room lies to the left at the bottom of the staircase; the guard inside will sometimes leave to patrol the lobby. Players can wait or intimidate him if they wish to disable the cameras.

The computer is in the center of office B2, directly below a camera. Another guard patrols the area, along with a civilian janitor.

Time to escape

Heisters escape via the window in the closet upstairs.

Consequences

If Baker completes the mission successfully, he acquires the Torini 3000 for the current playthrough. Otherwise, he must purchase documentation for the car from Clutch, who offers to join Baker’s crew (if he hasn’t done so before). Failing this mission is the only way to unlock Clutch in Baker’s Battle.

If the player interacted with the computer—even he then fails the mission—Nasara will examine the DMV data, find armored truck registrations, and suggest robbing the trucks.
