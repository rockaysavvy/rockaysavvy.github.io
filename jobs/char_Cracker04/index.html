On Rails is the fourth mission in Cracker’s story. After Cracker secures the Wolf B’s flight path data, the Scientist uses it to locate the Bomb—buried deep in the Arctic. Nasara dispatches Cracker to oversee its excavation and exfiltration.

Strategy

Completing the initial objectives as fast as possible gives Cracker’s backup a better chance of surviving the waves of Specnaz. Cracker’s Chopper only has a 20-round magazine, so players may want to switch to semiautomatic to minimize reloads or use his secondary Brescia 92, which reloads faster.

Stay on guard

Cracker begins the mission on a cliff edge; the fall is lethal. Several Specnaz appear below, one of whom launches an RPG at Cracker and his compatriot.

Cracker can replenish his ammunition using the crates mounted on either side of the sled. Players should quickly kill the Specnaz who appear on the ridge and behind the sled, since their numbers can overwhelm Cracker and his backup.

Occasionally the sled’s engine may flood; Cracker must hold out while it rests, then restart it.

Hold off the enemies

The ice mounds on the floe offer good cover for Cracker. After he kills the remaining 15–20 Specnaz, he can escape by submarine.

Consequences

Completing this mission satisfies the Scientist. He reveals to Nasara that the Nemesis took the briefcase to Tora Bora to sell to Lafoushini at the behest of the CIA. Nasara sends Cracker to intercept the exchange, exact his vengeance and recover the briefcase. If Cracker fails or ignores the mission, he dies, and his story ends.

Trivia

  • The mission’s name, “on rails,” is a critique often lobbed at video games with linear progression and little to no exploration. Many of the game’s character missions adopt this modality intentionally.
