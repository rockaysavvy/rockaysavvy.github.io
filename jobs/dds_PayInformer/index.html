Pay the Informer is a story trade and the first mission in A Rich Guest, part of the plot line.

Silver explains that the Zang family is moving to Rockay City along with their enormous fortune. Among their assets are the Dragon Dogs, a pair of ancient Mongolian statues. She asks for $25,000 in exchange for detailed information on the Dogs’ whereabouts once they enter the city.
