Bank Heist is a type of moneymaking mission.

Scenarios

Default
Heisters begin outside the bank; the alarm has not been triggered.
Zeroed in
Like the default scenario, but all computer locations are marked with waypoints.
Eye in the sky
Like the default scenario, but all guards are permanently highlighted.
Silent vault
Heisters begin inside the vault; the alarm has not been triggered.
Under control
Heisters begin in the back hallway and already have the code to open the vault. The alarm has not been raised.
Cracking
Heisters begin directly outside the vault; the alarm has already been raised and the drill placed on the vault.
Chopper & bags
Heisters begin on the roof of the building. All the loot has been bagged, the alarm has been raised, and the escape is by helicopter.
