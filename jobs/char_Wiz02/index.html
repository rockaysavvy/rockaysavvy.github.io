Wiz’s story

Soap Up

“Where you at, pretty boy?”

Check Mate

CrewWiz

StealthRequired

Soap Up is the third mission in FTD WizDisc and a mission in Wiz’s alternative story. It’s only available if Baker successfully plants the snooping device at Rockay Deposit Reserve.

Wiz decrypts the disk using data from the sniffer and decides to smuggle its contents out of Rockay Plains Correction Facility inside a bar of soap. He pays Santos $200,000 to smuggle the soap out, but several prison guards interrupt their meeting before he can hand it over.

Strategy

If a guard’s detection meter fills at any point, the mission instantly ends in failure. Guards grow suspicious if they see Wiz or the soap, but not if they see open vents.

Pick up the soap

The soap slips out of Wiz’s hand every 6–8 seconds. Press to pick it up and to drop it.

Hide the soap

Wiz must drop the soap before he interacts with the vents in the showers. All but four vents are jammed, so players must keep checking vents until one opens. When it does, they should press to stash the soap inside, then close the vent .

Consequences

After Wiz protects the soap from the guards, Santos keeps his word and transports it out of Rockay Plains Correction Facility. Its contents allow Gloves to locate the Hidden Vault and plan the heist for Baker.

If the player ignores or fails the mission, Nasara finds another way to decode the disk in 3–4 days.
