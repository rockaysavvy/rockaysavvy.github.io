What’s on the Boat?

Rescue Khan’s Dealer

“Free Khan’s man—force him to tell us about the vessel.”

Crew1–4 heisters

StealthSupported

Rescue Khan’s Dealer is an optional mission in What’s on the Boat?; it’s only available if Hielo is still in Rockay City when the plot line starts. After the Chicos kidnap Khan’s senior dealer to question him about the Miss Olga, Nasara suggests that Baker “liberate” him and do the interrogation himself. Players who have the cash should choose Casey’s plan instead and set up a Fake Coke Deal, since there is no risk involved and Baker keeps his money regardless.

Strategy

Chicos patrol throughout the warehouse: near the entrance, on the warehouse floor, on the mezzanine, and in the room with Khan’s dealer. If the alarm is raised, several more Chicos will arrive near the boathouse.

Consequences

If Baker’s crew frees Khan’s dealer successfully, Baker interrogates him, learns of the Olga’s imminent arrival, and proceeds with the heist. If they fail, he follows Casey’s plan and sets up a fake drug deal instead.
