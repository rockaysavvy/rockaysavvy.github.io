Melter Drill

This article is unfinished. It may contain incomplete or inaccurate information.

Melter Drill is an optional story trade mission in Piggy Bank. Baker can purchase a faster drill for the vault, so his crew spends less time waiting for it to open. The upgrade only costs $15,600, so players should purchase it if they don’t plan on completing the heist stealthily.
