Armored Truck Heist is a type of moneymaking mission in campaign and multiplayer. Baker sends his crew to intercept an armored truck carrying money or precious metals, break into the truck, and escape with the loot.

Strategy

Break into the armored car

Some mission variations, including Armored Truck II in Hauling Ash, present the crew with two trucks, which they can saw into concurrently:

Break into the armored cars

Campaign players can determine the number of trucks by the mission name—only “Transport of Riches” and “Money Convoy” have multiple trucks. In multiplayer, the mission description mentions that “police units have been tightly monitoring” the truck’s route.

Occasionally, Baker’s crew ambushes the truck as it is being laden:

Ambush the guards

The back door is open, and players can subdue the security guards and maintain stealth for the duration of the mission. Players can identify this variation in all game modes by checking the mission description for the word “loading” or “loaded.”
