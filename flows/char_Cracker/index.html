Cracker’s story

“Hammer, this is Nail.”

Cracker’s story is a character story in Baker’s Battle. It follows Nasara’s old acquaintance Nigel Thorne, an experienced ex–intelligence agent, as he finds his footing in the world of unsanctioned ops, encounters a worthy adversary, and teams up with an unscrupulous character to exact revenge.

Objectives

Two to four days elapse.

If the player began Cracker’s story in a previous campaign playthrough, it resumes. If the player finished his story, Cracker enters the hiring pool fully promoted.

Consequences

Completing Cracker’s story unlocks the achievement Cracked the Jack. Cracker also offers to join Baker’s crew and is promoted to level 4; contrary to the final cutscene, he cannot use the shoulder-mounted gun he found in the briefcase. He remains fully promoted in the hiring pool in future campaign playthroughs until he is killed or arrested, at which point his story restarts.

If Cracker dies before he completes his story, it resumes in the next campaign playthrough.
