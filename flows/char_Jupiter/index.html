Jupiter’s story

“Love you too, M.”

Jupiter’s story is a character story in Baker’s Battle. It follows Jupiter and her father as they grow their fitness and drug businesses in Rockay City. Things soon spiral out of control, and their saga intersects with Baker’s campaign to control the city.

Consequences

Completing Jupiter’s story unlocks the achievement Olympus Ascended, and Jupiter offers to join Baker’s crew. She remains in the hiring pool in future campaign playthroughs until she is killed or arrested, at which point her story restarts.

If Jupiter dies before she completes her story, detectives Donny and Phips return to the streets of Rockay. Her story resumes in the next campaign playthrough.
