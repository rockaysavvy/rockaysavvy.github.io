Dave

“Wazzup Coach? I hear you need somethin’…”

This article is unfinished. It may contain incomplete or inaccurate information.

Dave is a foreman who works for Wild Construction. He is Touchdown’s primary contact for development and maintenance work.

Appearances

  • Dave leads the window cleaning crew that sets up the zipline in the Hidden Vault heist.
  • He offsers to sell an improved buzzsaw to Baker for his Money Train robbery. Wild’s contract with Central Hospital & Clinics also provides the opportunity for Gloves to set up the getaway.
  • He plants the rappel gear and operates the crane when Baker steals the Dragon Dogs.

Trivia

  • Touchdown often calls him “Davey,” implying they know each other well.
