Arms Dealer

“Oh man, oh gosh…. I got enough guns! I don’t need this!”

The Arms Dealer sells higher-quality weapons than the black market and is the only way players can purchase epic and legendary weapons in Baker’s Battle. However, he has less inventory and requires Baker to have money available before he agrees to a meeting. Players can access the Arms Dealer by clicking his icon on the map.

Strategy

The Arms Dealer only appears if Baker has enough cash at the beginning of the day, and he never makes an offer on the first day of the campaign. His market remains open for two days or until Baker purchases his entire inventory; then he closes up shop and takes three days to restock with a new, randomized selection.
