Servo

“Guess who’s controlling the robots now, motherfucker!”

This article is unfinished. It may contain incomplete or inaccurate information.

Servomechaniker, or Servo for short, is a pseudonymous hacker and revolutionary in Rockay City. The former CTO of Cagnali Industries, he grows disillusioned with the company’s direction and works with Baker to dismantle Cagnali’s Order.

First appearance

Servo interrupted IGS Dev Stream 17 and ended it prematurely to publicize his #RESISTORDER movement. In the same day, he hacked the Cagnali Industries website, the IGS website, and Rockay Savvy itself, replacing much of the content with #RESISTORDER branding.

Campaign

After Cagnali’s forces swam Rockay City and arrest a sizable portion of Baker’s army, Servo contacts Baker with a plan to take down Cagnali and her robot army.
