Gang bosses

This article is unfinished. It may contain incomplete or inaccurate information.

The gang bosses are powerful figures in Rockay City’s political and economic scene. They manage illegal empires and vie for control of the city’s neighborhoods and businesses amid the power vacuum that the King’s death created.

  • Travis Baker, leader of the Graves
  • Hielo, leader of the Chicos
  • Dollar Dragon, leader of the Sicarios
  • Don Barbaro, former leader of the Scudos
  • Cagnali, current leader of the Scudos
  • Karl Kong, leader of the Riders and better known as Khan
