Eduardo Reyes, better known as Dollar Dragon, is the gang boss who leads the Sicarios. The King’s former right-hand man, he strikes out on his own after the party explosion decimates his network, his assets, and his former boss.

Unlike the other bosses vying for control of Rockay City, Reyes values honor, respect, and machismo. Even when he joins forces with Baker, their philosophical differences, combined with their hot tempers, create tension and disputes.

Trivia

  • People call him “Dollar Dragon” because Reyes has a tattoo of dollar bills in the shape of a dragon across his chest.
