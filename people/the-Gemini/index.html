the Gemini

“You know our price… for the blades and the fuel.”

This article is unfinished. It may contain incomplete or inaccurate information.

The Gemini are licensed helicopter pilots who work frequently with Baker and his crew.

Appearances

  • Baker hires the Gemini to transport his crew after they rob the Money Train and to extract both Dragon Dogs after he secures them from the vaults at Imperial Bank.
  • Touchdown pays the Gemini to bomb Cagnali’s datacenters in Cyberstorm.
  • Wiz hires a Gemini to fly him and his cash reserves out of the country in Spend Money To Make Money.
  • After one of the Gemini is captured by the RCPD, Baker’s crew rescues him to hamper the Sheriff’s investigation.
