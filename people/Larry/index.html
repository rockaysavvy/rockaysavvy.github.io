Larry the Jeweler

“Jew-Larry—Clever…. I like you.”

This article is unfinished. It may contain incomplete or inaccurate information.

Larry the Jeweler is Nasara’s contact for jewelry and specialty tools. He sells an engagement ring which Gloves trades for information on the Money Train, as well as a faster drill which Baker’s crew uses to break into the Hidden Vault.

Trivia

  • Some call him “Larry the Jew” (a pun on “jewelery”), but he dislikes this moniker.
