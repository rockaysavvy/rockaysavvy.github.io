Milo

This article is unfinished. It may contain incomplete or inaccurate information.

Milo is a lynx in Rockay City. His whereabouts are currently unknown.

Appearances

  • Milo hasn’t appeared yet, but a notice asking after him has been posted outside of the Rockay City Police Department.

Trivia

  • Milo is named after InGame Studios employee Miloslav “Lynx” Cinko, the game’s Lead Producer.
  • He shares his name with University of Colorado Denver mascot Milo the Lynx.
  • Milo also makes a cameo on the cover of the Smithsonian Institution’s Zoology: Inside the Secret World of Animals.
